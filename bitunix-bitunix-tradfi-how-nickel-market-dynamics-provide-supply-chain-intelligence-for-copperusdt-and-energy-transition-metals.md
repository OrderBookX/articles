## Nickel's Battery-to-Stainless Demand Transition

Nickel was historically dominated by stainless steel demand (60-70% of consumption). EV batteries have changed this: high-nickel battery chemistries (NMC, NCA) require significant nickel as a cathode material. As EV penetration grows, battery demand now represents 15-20% of nickel consumption and is growing rapidly.
