# Crypto Futures Grid Bot — Altcoin Grids vs Major Pair Grids

BTC and ETH are the default grid trading pairs, and for good reason. But altcoins offer a different risk-reward profile that experienced grid traders sometimes exploit. Here's an honest comparison.

## Major Pair Advantages

BTC/USDT and ETH/USDT have the deepest liquidity, tightest spreads, most predictable oscillation patterns, and the most available analysis for identifying ranges. Slippage is minimal. Fills are reliable 24/7. Your grid operates in an environment optimized for high-frequency automated strategies.

## Altcoin Opportunities

Some altcoins oscillate in tighter percentage ranges with higher frequency than BTC. A mid-cap token ranging 8% daily with consistent back-and-forth can produce more grid cycles per day than BTC ranging 2%. If liquidity is adequate, these extra cycles generate more profit.

### The Catch

Altcoin liquidity is rarely adequate for aggressive grid strategies. Wider spreads eat into per-cycle profit. Thinner order books cause slippage during fast moves. And altcoins are prone to sudden pumps or dumps driven by news, listings, or whale activity that can break ranges instantly — without the gradual breakout patterns BTC typically shows.

## Practical Guidelines for Altcoin Grids

If you do trade altcoin grids, stick to pairs with consistent daily volume above $50 million. Use wider ranges than you would for BTC to account for higher volatility. Lower your leverage further — 2x to 3x maximum. And always set tight stop-loss, because altcoin breakouts can be fast and severe.

## The Portfolio Approach

The safest approach: run your core grids on majors for consistent, reliable performance. Use altcoin grids as small satellite positions for potentially higher returns, accepting higher risk. Keep altcoin grid allocation below 20% of your total grid capital.

## When to Move to Altcoins

Only consider altcoin grids after running at least 10 successful major-pair grids and developing a reliable parameter selection process. The skills you build on BTC/USDT — range identification, leverage calibration, fee optimization — transfer directly to altcoin grids but with tighter constraints. You need that foundation before adding the extra complexity and risk that less liquid pairs bring to your grid portfolio.
