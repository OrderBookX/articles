## Free TradFi Intelligence Tools

Federal Reserve Data (FRED): The St. Louis Federal Reserve's FRED database provides free access to over 800,000 economic data series including TIPS yields, inflation data, manufacturing indices, and employment data. This is the primary free source for the real interest rate data that drives XAUUSDT and XAGUSDT analysis. Access at fred.stlouisfed.org.

CFTC Commitment of Traders: Published weekly every Friday afternoon, the COT report shows the positioning of commercial hedgers, managed money (hedge funds), and small speculators in all major commodity futures markets. When managed money is at extreme net long or short positions in gold or copper, contrarian signals are available. Free at cftc.gov.

LME Daily Inventory Reports: The London Metal Exchange publishes daily warehouse inventory levels for copper, aluminum, zinc, nickel, and other base metals. These reports are free on the LME website and provide the most direct physical market tightness signal for bitunix copper futures via COPPERUSDT.
