Risk Management Tools and Strategies
------------------------------------

To mitigate **[futures trading](https://www.bitunix.com?inviteCode=ab9nr3&utm_source=news&utm_medium=blog-article&utm_campaign=labsnews) risks**, traders must employ effective risk management strategies. Key tools include:

- **Position Sizing:** Adjusting the size of your trades in relation to your account balance to manage risk exposure.
- **Stop Loss Orders:** Automatically sell positions at a predefined price to limit potential losses.
- **Margin Requirements:** Understanding the maintenance and initial margin requirements to avoid unexpected liquidations.

The 2025 market was marked by extreme volatility, with open interest peaking at $235.9 billion on October 7 before a massive deleveraging event wiped out over $70 billion in positions. The sudden crash on October 10-11, triggered by geopolitical events, underscored the importance of these tools.

### Case Study: The October 2025 Crash

During the October 2025 crash, $19 billion+ in liquidations were recorded, with 85-90% from long positions. The crash was triggered by an unexpected announcement of 100% tariffs on Chinese imports, illustrating how **over-leveraging** can exacerbate market downturns.

The events of 2025 highlight several critical lessons for traders:

1. **Stay Informed:** Keeping abreast of geopolitical events and market trends can help anticipate market movements.
2. **Use Risk Management Tools:** Employing tools like stop loss orders and appropriate position sizing can mitigate losses.
3. **Understand Liquidity Risks:** High beta assets like Bitcoin can experience significant price swings, emphasizing the need for liquidity management.

Bitcoin demonstrated its nature as a high-beta asset with a 2.5-3.0 coefficient to global liquidity, surging from $40,000 to $126,000 during the Fed's rate-cutting cycle before the October correction.
