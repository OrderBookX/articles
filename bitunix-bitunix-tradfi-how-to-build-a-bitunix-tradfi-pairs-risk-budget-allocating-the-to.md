## The Factor-Level Budget Overlay

The instrument-level allocations must also respect factor-level concentration limits (from Article 329). Calculate the TIPS factor exposure of all current positions. If the TIPS factor exposure exceeds 12% of account equity (from XAUUSDT + XAGUSDT + XPTUSDT combined), the factor-level limit is approached even if each instrument is within its individual instrument budget.

The rule: instrument-level budgets and factor-level concentration limits are both binding constraints. A position that is within its instrument budget but breaches the factor concentration limit must be reduced.
