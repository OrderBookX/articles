## The Compliance Data Sources

Four independent sources provide OPEC production estimates that can be compared to announced targets:

1. IEA Monthly Oil Market Report (free executive summary): Publishes OPEC-10 and OPEC+ production estimates from multiple data sources including direct communication with member countries.

2. EIA STEO (Short-Term Energy Outlook, free monthly): EIA's independent estimate of OPEC production, derived from tanker tracking, export data, and direct country reporting.

3. S&P Global Platts OPEC Survey: Published monthly, aggregates estimates from tanker tracking firms, industry consultants, and direct country contacts. Freely quoted in financial media.

4. Rystad Energy Monthly: Premium service but key findings are published in financial media; tanker tracking-based production estimates.

When three or more of these sources agree on compliance direction (all showing above or below target production), the signal has high confidence. Discrepancy among sources indicates genuine uncertainty about actual production levels.
