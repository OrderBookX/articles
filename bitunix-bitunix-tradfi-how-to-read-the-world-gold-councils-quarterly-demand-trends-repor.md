## When the WGC Report Publishes and Where to Access It

The World Gold Council publishes its quarterly Gold Demand Trends approximately 6 weeks after each quarter ends: Q1 data in early May, Q2 data in early August, Q3 data in early November, Q4 data in late January/early February. The full report is free at gold.org/goldhub/research/gold-demand-trends and includes Excel data downloads for all demand categories.
