## The OPEC Meeting Calendar and Pre-Meeting Intelligence

OPEC holds formal ministerial meetings typically twice per year in June and December, with OPEC+ monitoring committee meetings throughout the year. These meetings are scheduled months in advance and serve as the primary production decision mechanism. Pre-meeting positioning in bitunix oil futures should begin two to three weeks before any scheduled OPEC meeting.

Pre-meeting intelligence gathering: Monitor statements from individual OPEC member countries' energy ministries in the weeks before the meeting. Saudi Arabia's statements are most informative, as it functions as the de facto swing producer. When Saudi officials signal concern about price levels or market balance, production cuts are more likely. When they signal satisfaction with current prices, production holds or increases are more likely.

## Trade the Expectation, Not Just the Event

The most profitable OPEC trading approach positions ahead of the widely expected outcome, not after it is announced. If market consensus expects a production cut of 500,000 barrels per day and a larger cut of 1 million barrels is announced, the move beyond the expected cut is the tradeable surprise. If the expected cut is confirmed exactly, much of the move has already been priced in during the pre-meeting positioning period.

For bitunix oil futures pre-meeting positioning: if pre-meeting intelligence suggests a larger-than-expected cut is likely, establish a modest long position in the week before the meeting.
