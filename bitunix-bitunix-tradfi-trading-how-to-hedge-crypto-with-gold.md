Hedging is a risk management technique borrowed from traditional finance. The concept is simple: take a position in an asset that moves in the opposite direction of your primary holding. ## Why Gold Works as a Crypto Hedge

Gold has historically served as a safe-haven asset during periods of economic uncertainty. When equity markets sell off, when inflation rises, or when geopolitical tensions escalate, capital flows into gold. Crypto markets, despite Bitcoin's occasional "digital gold" narrative, still behave more like risk assets during macro shocks. During the February 2026 crash, crypto prices dropped sharply while gold prices moved higher. This inverse relationship makes gold an effective hedge for crypto portfolios.
