## London: The Primary Physical Price Discovery Venue

The LBMA physical market in London handles the largest volume of physical gold trading globally   primarily through unallocated gold accounts (claims on gold held by bullion banks) and physical delivery from LBMA Good Delivery bars. The LBMA AM and PM benchmarks (discussed in an earlier article) are the global pricing reference.

London's hours (8 AM to 3 PM GMT) include the most liquid period of gold trading globally: the overlap between Asian session close and US pre-market. The highest-quality price discovery in the spot market occurs during London hours.

## New York (COMEX): The Primary Speculative Venue

COMEX gold futures represent the largest speculative and hedging venue for gold. The 100-ounce gold futures contract and the micro (10-ounce) contract are the instruments that institutional funds, hedge funds, and retail speculators use most extensively. COMEX open interest (the aggregate number of open futures contracts) is the primary measure of speculative positioning.

The key COMEX signal for XAUUSDT: when COMEX open interest is rising while the gold price is also rising (new speculative money entering the long side), the rally has sustainable fuel. When COMEX open interest is falling while gold is rising (short covering rather than new longs), the rally may be near its limit.
