## October-December: Pre-Year-End Inventory Optimization

Industrial copper consumers review their year-end inventory positions in Q4. Companies with excess inventory sell into the market; companies with insufficient inventory buy. This pre-year-end inventory adjustment creates term structure volatility in Q4 as different consumers have different inventory optimization directions.
