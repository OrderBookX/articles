## The CBDC Development Landscape

Over 130 central banks are exploring CBDCs in various stages from research to pilot to live deployment. The Digital Yuan (e-CNY) is the most advanced major economy CBDC with hundreds of millions of digital wallets and growing merchant acceptance. The European Central Bank's digital euro is in the preparation phase, targeting potential launch in the late 2020s. The US Federal Reserve has been more cautious, issuing research papers but facing political opposition to a retail CBDC.
