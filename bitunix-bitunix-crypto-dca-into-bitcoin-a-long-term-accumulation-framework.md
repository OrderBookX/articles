## When DCA Into Bitcoin Does Not Work

DCA fails if you abandon it during drawdowns. A 50% Bitcoin crash is not a signal to stop. It is the condition that makes DCA most effective because your fixed amount buys significantly more BTC per cycle. Stopping during crashes and restarting during rallies is the worst possible execution pattern. DCA also underperforms lump-sum investing if you have capital available and the market only goes up from your entry point. But since nobody can reliably predict that, DCA remains the safer approach for most investors.
