### Understanding Bitcoin Futures and Perpetual Contracts

Bitcoin futures are contracts that obligate the purchaser to buy Bitcoin at a predetermined price on a future date. However, the innovation of *perpetual contracts* allows traders to hold positions indefinitely, using **funding rates** to keep the contract price aligned with the spot price. This flexibility makes them attractive to both individual and institutional investors.
