## When Political Risk Premiums Are Insufficient: The Anticipatory Long

When political tension is building gradually but the commodity market has not yet priced any premium (price is at or below fundamental value), the anticipatory long captures the premium as the market belatedly recognizes the risk. This requires identifying political situations early in their development, before the mainstream financial media covers them as commodity market stories.

Sources for early political risk intelligence: regional foreign policy research organizations, think tank reports, specialized geopolitical intelligence services, and diplomatic news from countries involved in the potential risk event.
