Understanding Crypto Auto Invest
--------------------------------

Crypto auto invest is a strategy that allows investors to automate their cryptocurrency purchases, usually at regular intervals. This approach is akin to a systematic investment plan (SIP) in traditional finance and helps in building a *long-term portfolio* without the need for constant market monitoring. The concept leverages the power of **dollar-cost averaging (DCA)**, a technique that involves regular investments, regardless of price fluctuations, to mitigate the impact of market volatility.
