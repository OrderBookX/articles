Understanding Futures Trading
-----------------------------

Futures trading involves entering contracts to buy or sell assets at a predetermined price at a future date. Unlike [spot trading](%5BLINK%5D), where assets are exchanged immediately, futures contracts are agreements to transact in the future. This allows traders to speculate on the price direction of assets like Bitcoin without owning them outright.
