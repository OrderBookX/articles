## Allocating Between DCA and Copy Trading

A conservative integration might dedicate 80% of your crypto investment budget to DCA and 20% to copy trading. This ensures the majority of your capital is accumulating through the disciplined, lower-risk mechanism while a smaller portion seeks enhanced returns through active trading replication. Never allocate more to copy trading than you can afford to lose entirely, because even skilled traders experience significant drawdowns.
