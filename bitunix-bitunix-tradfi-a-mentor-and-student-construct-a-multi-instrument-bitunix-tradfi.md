**Student:** Okay. But once I'm ready to build the portfolio, what's the framework?

**Mentor:** Think in terms of risk allocation, not capital allocation. Don't ask "how much money do I put in each instrument?" Ask "how much daily volatility risk do I allocate to each instrument?"
