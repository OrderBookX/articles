## MicroStrategy in One Paragraph

MicroStrategy is a business intelligence software company that decided in 2020 to use Bitcoin as its primary treasury reserve asset. It has since accumulated hundreds of thousands of Bitcoin by issuing convertible notes and equity and deploying the proceeds into Bitcoin purchases. Today, MicroStrategy's stock price is primarily a function of its Bitcoin holdings' value rather than its software business. This makes MSTRUSDT essentially a leveraged Bitcoin equity proxy.

## Why MSTRUSDT Is Not the Same as Bitcoin

When Bitcoin is at $100,000 and MicroStrategy holds 500,000 Bitcoin, MicroStrategy's Bitcoin NAV is $50 billion. But MSTRUSDT may trade at $55 billion market cap (a 10% premium to NAV) or at $45 billion (a 10% discount). This premium or discount fluctuates based on institutional demand for equity-structured Bitcoin exposure, market sentiment, and the perceived sustainability of MicroStrategy's debt structure.

Bitunix mstr futures via MSTRUSDT therefore adds equity market risk (premium/discount to NAV) on top of Bitcoin price risk. During Bitcoin bull markets, MSTRUSDT often outperforms Bitcoin because the premium expands. During Bitcoin bear markets, MSTRUSDT often underperforms Bitcoin because the premium contracts or turns to discount.
