## The Source Reliability Tiers

Tier 1   Highest reliability (primary data, published by the responsible authority):
US EIA Petroleum Status Report: measured inventory and production data. High frequency (weekly), physically verifiable (storage tank measurements and production gauging), and publicly available immediately.
LME Warehouse Stock Reports: physically measured inventory in LME-certified warehouses. Direct physical measurement with third-party warehouse management.
CFTC Commitment of Traders: regulatory filing data   firms report their actual positions under legal obligation. Accurate to Tuesday close, with weekly frequency.
FRED Federal Reserve Economic Data: official government statistical releases aggregated by the St. Louis Fed.

Tier 2   High reliability (derived data from Tier 1 sources):
World Gold Council Gold Demand Trends: derived from central bank reserve reporting, ETF custodian data, and industry surveys. Well-established methodology, but some components (OTC investment, technology demand) rely on modeled estimates.
Johnson Matthey PGM Market Report: industry leader with direct commercial relationships across the PGM supply chain. Annual frequency limits timeliness.
ICSG Copper Bulletin: compiles national statistics with known reporting lags. Data is accurate when eventually published but can be revised.

Tier 3   Moderate reliability (survey-based, modeled, or delayed data):
PMI surveys: qualitative surveys that may not accurately capture actual production volumes. Well-correlated with actual industrial output but subject to survey bias.
Analyst consensus estimates: aggregations of modeled forecasts. Consensus beats and misses are relative to this modeled expectation, not to physical reality.

## The Conflict Resolution Rule

When Tier 1 and Tier 3 sources conflict (e.g., EIA inventory shows crude building while PMI shows expansion): weight the Tier 1 physical data more heavily. Physical inventory data measures actual behavior; survey data measures perceived behavior. When behavior and perception diverge, behavior is more likely to be correct.
