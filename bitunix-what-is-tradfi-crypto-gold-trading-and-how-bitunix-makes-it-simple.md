## Reading the Gold Market

Successful gold trading requires understanding a different set of indicators than crypto. The US Dollar Index (DXY) has an inverse relationship with gold: when the dollar strengthens, gold tends to weaken, and vice versa. Real interest rates, which are nominal rates minus inflation, drive gold's opportunity cost. When real rates are negative, gold becomes more attractive because holding it costs nothing relative to bonds. Federal Reserve policy statements, inflation data (CPI and PCE), and employment reports are the key catalysts for gold price movements.
