## Partial Hedging: Protecting Without Full Neutralization

Full hedges eliminate all directional exposure, which also eliminates upside potential. Partial hedging protects a portion of your BAT crypto position while maintaining some exposure to potential gains. A 50 percent hedge means half your BAT position is protected while the other half participates in any continued upside.

Adjust your hedge ratio based on conviction. Lower conviction about near-term direction warrants a larger hedge. Higher conviction warrants a smaller hedge or no hedge at all. Bitunix futures allow precise sizing to match your desired hedge percentage.
