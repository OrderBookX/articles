## What Is TradFi's EM Capital Flow Mechanism?

What is TradFi's EM capital outflow trigger? When US rates rise significantly above EM rates, global capital flows toward US dollar assets seeking higher yields. This capital outflow depreciates EM currencies, raises local interest rates (to defend currencies), and reduces domestic investment and consumption.
