## What Funding Rates Are and Why They Exist

Perpetual futures contracts on Bitunix have no expiration date, which means there is no natural settlement mechanism to keep the futures price aligned with the Ankr crypto spot price. Funding rates fill this role by creating periodic payments between long and short traders that incentivize the futures price to stay close to spot.

When the futures price trades above spot (positive funding), long position holders pay short position holders. This payment discourages excessive long positioning and pushes the futures price back toward spot. When futures trades below spot (negative funding), shorts pay longs, discouraging excessive short positioning.

Funding payments on Bitunix occur every eight hours. The rate varies based on the premium or discount between ANKR futures and spot prices. Rates can be positive, negative, or very close to zero depending on market sentiment and positioning.

## How Funding Rates Affect Your ANKR token Positions

If you hold a long ANKR futures position during a period of consistently positive funding, you pay the funding rate every eight hours. These costs accumulate and reduce your effective returns. A position that shows a 5 percent unrealized gain may only deliver 4 percent after accounting for funding payments over a multi-day holding period.

Conversely, if you hold a short position during positive funding, you receive payments every eight hours. Experienced traders sometimes take positions specifically to capture funding income when rates are elevated, treating it as a yield strategy rather than a directional bet.

## Using Funding Rates as a Trading Signal

Extreme funding rates often precede market reversals because they indicate crowded positioning. When Ankr crypto funding is extremely positive, the market is heavily long, and a correction that liquidates overleveraged longs becomes more likely. When funding is extremely negative, the market is heavily short, and a short squeeze becomes more probable.

For short-term trades lasting hours to a day or two, funding rates have minimal impact on ANKR position economics. The directional move typically dominates.
