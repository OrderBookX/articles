## The Strategic Motivation Behind SWF Commodity Allocation

SWFs typically originate from three sources: commodity-exporting countries (Norway's Government Pension Fund from oil revenues, Abu Dhabi Investment Authority from oil), trade surplus countries (China Investment Corporation from export surpluses), and fiscal stabilization programs (Chile's Economic and Social Stabilization Fund from copper revenues).

Their commodity allocation motivation is often counter-cyclical by design: when their primary commodity revenue source is high (oil prices elevated for GCC SWFs), they are accumulating financial assets including gold as diversification. When the primary commodity revenue falls, they draw down financial asset reserves rather than cutting government spending.
