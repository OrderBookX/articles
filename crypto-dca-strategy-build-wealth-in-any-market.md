Understanding the Crypto DCA Strategy
-------------------------------------

The *Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)* strategy involves investing a fixed amount of money at regular intervals, regardless of the asset’s price. This approach minimizes the impact of volatility and removes the emotional decision-making often associated with trying to time the market.

- **Market-Proof Strategy:** DCA is designed to perform in both **bull and bear markets**, making it an **all-weather investing** tactic.
- **Wealth Building:** By investing consistently over time, investors can accumulate assets and potentially increase their **market cap** share.
- **Reduced Risk:** DCA reduces the risk of investing a large sum at an inopportune time, thus avoiding the pitfalls of **drawdown**.
