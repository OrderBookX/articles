## Identifying Valid Support and Resistance

A level is valid when price has touched it at least twice. Three touches makes it strong. On Bitunix charts, draw horizontal lines at these levels. The zone between the lowest valid support and the highest valid resistance is your candidate grid range.

### Setting Range Inside the Extremes

Do not set your grid at the absolute high and low. Set it slightly inside — maybe 2 to 3% inward from each extreme. This accounts for wicks that briefly exceed the range without representing true breakouts. On Bitunix, this approach keeps the bot active in the zone where 80% of price action occurs.
