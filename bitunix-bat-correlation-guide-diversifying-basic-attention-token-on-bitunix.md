## What Correlation Means for BAT coin Investors

Correlation measures how closely two assets move together. Perfectly correlated assets (correlation of 1.0) move in lockstep, providing zero diversification benefit. Uncorrelated assets (correlation near 0) move independently, reducing portfolio volatility when combined. Negatively correlated assets (correlation below 0) move in opposite directions, providing maximum diversification benefit.

BAT has its own correlation profile relative to Bitcoin, other altcoins, traditional equities, bonds, gold, and commodities. Understanding these relationships helps you determine how much BAT crypto to add and what it replaces or complements in your existing portfolio.

However, during extreme risk-off events, correlations across all asset classes tend to spike as investors sell everything for cash. This correlation convergence during crises is important to account for in your BAT position sizing.
