## Why Neutral Rate Environments Are Challenging for XAUUSDT

XAUUSDT's primary analytical framework is built around the direction of TIPS yields. When TIPS yields are moving   falling in an easing cycle or rising in a tightening cycle   the direction provides a clear fundamental signal. When TIPS yields are stable for weeks or months (the Fed on hold), the primary signal is absent and secondary signals must carry more analytical weight.

The common mistake: practitioners who are accustomed to TIPS-led analysis become frustrated during neutral rate periods and either force trades on insufficient signal or exit the market entirely. Both are suboptimal.
