Advantages: better average entry price than immediately buying the confirmed signal. Adds the technical discipline of entering at demonstrated support.

Disadvantages: if the pullback does not occur   if prices immediately continue higher from the fundamental confirmation signal   the practitioner misses the move entirely while waiting for a technical level that never materializes.

## Method 3: Breakout Entry With Confirmation

Enter when the price breaks above a key resistance level (52-week high, prior major resistance, round number) with increased volume. The breakout signals that the market consensus has shifted from "uncertain" to "confirming the bull thesis."

Disadvantages: entering at breakouts means entering at the highest price of the prior period. If the breakout fails (breaks out and immediately reverses), the stop is the prior resistance level which is often very close to the entry   creating a poor risk-reward profile.
