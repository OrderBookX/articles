## Cost Basis Methods for ATOM crypto

Your cost basis is what you paid for each ATOM token, and it determines your taxable gain or loss when you sell. Several methods exist for calculating cost basis: First In First Out (FIFO), Last In First Out (LIFO), and Specific Identification. Different jurisdictions allow different methods, and the choice can significantly affect your tax liability.

If you use Bitunix HODL for dollar-cost averaging into Atom, each recurring purchase creates a separate tax lot with its own cost basis. Tracking each lot individually provides the most accurate tax calculations and the flexibility to choose the most tax-efficient lots when selling.
