## The Earnings Week Options Flow Pattern

In the week before Tesla earnings, options market participants establish positions for the anticipated move. The size of open interest in out-of-the-money calls and puts at various strikes reveals what move size the market is expecting and which direction institutional hedgers are more actively protecting.

When put open interest significantly exceeds call open interest at strikes equidistant from the current price, the options market is pricing more downside protection than upside speculation. This pattern often precedes downside earnings surprises because institutional holders who are protecting large TSLAUSDT longs purchase put hedges before potential bad news.

Conversely, when call open interest significantly exceeds put open interest (more upside speculation than downside protection), the institutional setup is more bullish.
