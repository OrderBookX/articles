## Oil and the Hurricane Premium

US Gulf of Mexico oil production represents approximately 15-17% of total US crude production. During hurricane season (June through November, peak intensity August-October), tropical storms and hurricanes can force evacuation of offshore platforms and shutdown of production. The Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) publishes daily offshore production shut-in data during storm events (free at bsee.gov).
