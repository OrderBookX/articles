Report 2: Trade Quality Score Report. For each closed trade in the month, calculate a trade quality score from 1-10 based on process adherence: was the pre-trade journal entry completed (2 points), was the stop-loss placed at entry (2 points), was the position sized according to the two-step method (2 points), was the partial profit exit taken at the planned first target (2 points), and was the trade rationale aligned with the current macro regime (2 points)? Report 3: Instrument Return Attribution vs Benchmark. Compare each instrument's return in your account to what a passive leveraged position (same average leverage, no active timing) would have generated. The difference is your active management alpha. Consistently positive alpha in an instrument confirms genuine edge; consistently negative alpha suggests the instrument is better held passively or avoided.
