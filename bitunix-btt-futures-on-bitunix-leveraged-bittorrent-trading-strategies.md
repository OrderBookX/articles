## Essential Order Types for BTT crypto Futures

Stop-loss orders exit your position automatically at a predefined loss level. Set these before or immediately after every entry. Trading without a stop-loss on leveraged positions is the fastest path to account destruction.

Take-profit orders lock in gains at predetermined targets. Combined with stop-loss, they create a risk-reward bracket that automates your exit strategy.

Trailing stops follow price in your favor and trigger when price reverses by a set amount. For trending BTT moves, trailing stops capture the bulk of the move while protecting accumulated profits.

Reduce-only orders ensure that closing orders can only decrease your position size, preventing accidental additions during volatile execution.
