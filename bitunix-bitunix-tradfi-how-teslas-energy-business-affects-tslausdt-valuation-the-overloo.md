## The Energy Business Structure

Tesla's energy business has two components:

Energy Storage (Megapack and Powerwall): Large-scale battery storage systems sold to utilities, grid operators, and commercial customers (Megapack), and home battery storage systems paired with rooftop solar (Powerwall). Megapack is the primary growth driver   each unit stores approximately 3.9 MWh and is priced at approximately $1.5-2M per unit.

Solar Generation (Solar Roof and solar panels): Rooftop solar installations for residential customers. This segment generates less revenue than energy storage but creates a recurring service relationship with homeowners.
