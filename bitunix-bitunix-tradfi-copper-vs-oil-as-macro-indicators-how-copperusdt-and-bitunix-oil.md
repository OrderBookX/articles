## What Copper Signals vs What Oil Signals

COPPERUSDT is primarily a manufacturing and construction demand indicator. When Chinese factories increase production, copper is purchased before factory output appears in statistics. When construction starts accelerate, copper wiring and plumbing is purchased before building completions show up in GDP. Copper leads industrial activity by 4-8 weeks.

Bitunix oil futures is primarily a transportation and broad energy demand indicator. Oil consumption rises when economic activity increases travel (jet fuel, diesel), commuting (gasoline), and general energy consumption. Oil is slightly less "leading" than copper because energy demand responds to activity that is already occurring rather than being purchased in advance of activity.
