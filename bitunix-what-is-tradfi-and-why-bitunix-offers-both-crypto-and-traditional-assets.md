# What Is TradFi and Why Bitunix Offers Both Crypto and Traditional Assets

The question of what TradFi is comes up whenever crypto traders encounter the term in market discussions, exchange announcements, or financial news. At its core, TradFi is the abbreviation for traditional finance, the centralized financial system that includes banks, stock exchanges, commodity markets, insurance companies, and regulatory bodies. It is the system most people interact with daily, from checking account transactions to retirement fund contributions.

## Why a Crypto Exchange Would Offer TradFi Products

They wanted exposure to assets with different risk profiles, different market drivers, and different behavioral patterns during economic cycles. Precious metals fit this need perfectly. Gold has been a store of value for millennia, and its price dynamics are driven by inflation expectations and central bank policy rather than crypto-specific sentiment.
