Tokenization is the process of representing real-world assets as digital tokens on a blockchain. This concept sits at the exact intersection of TradFi and DeFi, taking assets from the traditional financial system and making them accessible through decentralized infrastructure. ## How Tokenization Bridges Both Worlds

In TradFi, owning gold means holding physical bullion, gold ETF shares, or commodity futures contracts through a licensed broker. In DeFi, tokenized gold has existed through projects that back tokens with physical reserves. Perpetual contracts offer a third approach: synthetic exposure that tracks the price of the underlying asset without requiring physical backing or token creation. This approach is the most capital-efficient and accessible method for active traders.
