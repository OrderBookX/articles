## The Rebalancing Concept Applied to Bitunix TradFi

In a traditional investment portfolio, rebalancing means restoring the intended weights (60% stocks, 40% bonds) when price movements drift the actual weights (75% stocks, 25% bonds after an equity bull market). The rebalancing sells equities (expensive after the rally) and buys bonds (cheap after underperformance)   creating a counter-cyclical discipline.
