Understanding Liquidation Cascade
---------------------------------

A **liquidation cascade** occurs when a rapid price drop triggers a series of forced liquidations, further driving down the asset price. This phenomenon is often seen during a *market downturn*, leading to **panic selling** and **capitulation**. Understanding this concept is vital for traders using [margin trading](https://www.bitunix.com/register?vipCode=BITUNIXBONUS&utm_source=3rdparty&utm_medium=blog-article&utm_campaign=labsnews) platforms like Bitunix.
