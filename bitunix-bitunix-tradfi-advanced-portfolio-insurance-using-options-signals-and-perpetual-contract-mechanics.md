## The Gamma Hedge Concept Applied to Perpetuals

Options gamma measures how quickly delta (directional sensitivity) changes as price moves. A high-gamma hedge becomes more hedging as price moves further adverse, providing increasing protection during large moves. Perpetuals do not have gamma by definition, but a similar scaling effect can be implemented manually: establish a small initial hedge that you add to as the primary position moves adversely.

If COPPERUSDT falls 5% and XAUUSDT has not fully offset the loss, add $1,500 more XAUUSDT (increasing the hedge as the adverse move develops).
