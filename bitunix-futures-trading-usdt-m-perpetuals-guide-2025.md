Margin and Risk Management
--------------------------

Effective risk management is crucial when trading with high leverage. Bitunix provides a robust framework with its **cross margin** and **isolated margin** options. The cross margin mode utilizes the entire balance of a trader's account to prevent liquidation, while the isolated margin mode limits the risk to the margin allocated to a specific position.

### Order Types on Bitunix

Successfully navigating the futures market requires a keen understanding of various **order types**. Bitunix supports a range of advanced orders to enhance strategy implementation:

- **Market Order:** Execute trades instantly at the best available price.
- **Stop Limit Order:** Automate selling to mitigate losses by setting a trigger price.
- **OCO Order:** Combine two orders to bracket risk and targets effectively.
