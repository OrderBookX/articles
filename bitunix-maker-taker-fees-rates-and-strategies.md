Understanding Maker and Taker Fees
----------------------------------

The **maker fee rate** and **taker fee rate** are two types of trading fees that apply to transactions in cryptocurrency exchanges. The *maker fee* is typically lower as it provides liquidity to the market by placing orders that are not immediately matched, whereas the *taker fee* is charged when an order is executed immediately, removing liquidity from the market.
