## Selecting Altcoins for Recurring Investment

Not every altcoin belongs in a DCA plan. The assets you automate purchases for should meet specific criteria. They should have sufficient liquidity so your orders execute cleanly. They should have active development and a clear use case. And most importantly, you should believe in their long-term viability enough to hold through a major drawdown. Meme coins, hype-driven tokens, and projects without clear fundamentals are not DCA candidates. They are trading vehicles. There is a difference.

Unlike Bitcoin, which most DCA investors plan to hold indefinitely, altcoin allocations may need periodic review. Projects lose relevance, teams fail to deliver, and ecosystems shift.
