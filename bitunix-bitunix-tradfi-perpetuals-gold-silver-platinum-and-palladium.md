## Understanding Each TradFi Asset

Gold (XAUUSDT) is the most recognized store of value in financial history. It serves as a hedge against inflation, currency weakness, and systemic risk. Institutional portfolios typically allocate between 5 and 15 percent to gold as a defensive position.

Silver (XAGUSDT) combines the characteristics of a precious metal with industrial demand. It is used extensively in electronics, solar panels, and medical equipment. This dual nature makes silver more volatile than gold but also creates more trading opportunities during industrial expansion or contraction cycles.

Platinum (XPTUSDT) and palladium (XPDUSDT) are primarily driven by industrial demand, especially from the automotive industry where they are used in catalytic converters. Supply constraints from major producing regions can create significant price movements independent of broader commodity trends.
