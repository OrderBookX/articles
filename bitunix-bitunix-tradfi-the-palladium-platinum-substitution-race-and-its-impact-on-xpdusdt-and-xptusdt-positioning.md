## Why Substitution Is Happening

Palladium and platinum are both platinum-group metals that catalyze the oxidation reactions in automotive catalytic converters. Palladium has historically dominated gasoline engine applications because of its superior performance at the temperatures typical of gasoline combustion. Platinum dominates diesel engine applications.

When the palladium price rose to three times the platinum price (which occurred between 2018-2021), automotive manufacturers had a strong economic incentive to invest in reformulating their catalyst recipes to use platinum where palladium had been used.
