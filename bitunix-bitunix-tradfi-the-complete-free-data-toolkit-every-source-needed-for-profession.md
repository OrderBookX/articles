## Monetary and Macroeconomic Data (FRED, CME, ECB)

Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED): fred.stlouisfed.org
Key series: DFII10 (10-year TIPS yield), T10YIE (10-year TIPS breakeven), DXY (dollar index), M2SL (US money supply), EFFR (Fed funds rate), UNRATE (unemployment), and hundreds more. Free, updated daily, downloadable as CSV.

CME FedWatch Tool: cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html
Implied Fed funds rate probability by meeting date. Updated in real-time. Free.

ECB Statistical Data Warehouse: sdw.ecb.europa.eu
Eurozone monetary data, interest rates, inflation. Free with registration.

CFTC Commitment of Traders: cftc.gov/MarketReports/CommitmentsofTraders
Weekly COT data for all CFTC-regulated futures. Free, updated weekly. The Legacy Futures report is the most useful for individual instrument positioning.
