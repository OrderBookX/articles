Conservative: 5 to 10% of allocated margin. Achievable in most ranging conditions within one to two weeks. Aggressive: 15 to 20%. Requires strong oscillation and favorable conditions. Unrealistic: 50%+. If your TP target requires perfect conditions for months, it will likely never trigger.

Set SL at the maximum amount you are willing to lose on this specific strategy. 20 to 30% of allocated margin is common — tight enough to prevent disaster, loose enough for normal grid fluctuations before recovery.
