## Essential Indicators for AXS token Analysis

Moving averages form the foundation of most Axie Infinity chart setups. The 20-period exponential moving average (EMA) tracks short-term momentum. The 50-period EMA identifies intermediate trend. The 200-period EMA defines the long-term trend. When shorter EMAs cross above longer ones, it signals potential bullish momentum. When they cross below, bearish pressure may be building.

Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures whether AXS is overbought (above 70) or oversold (below 30). Extreme readings often precede reversals, while RSI divergences where price makes a new high but RSI does not can signal weakening momentum before the price chart confirms it.

Volume analysis confirms price movements. Rising volume on advances confirms bullish conviction. Rising volume on declines confirms selling pressure. Low volume moves in either direction are less trustworthy and more likely to reverse.
