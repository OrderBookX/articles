## How TradFi Regulations Influence Crypto

KYC and AML requirements, which originated in banking regulation, are now standard across major crypto exchanges. Proof of Reserves, modeled after bank auditing requirements, has become an expectation following the FTX collapse. Capital requirements, similar to those applied to traditional brokers, are being considered for crypto exchanges in multiple jurisdictions. The direction is clear: crypto regulation is being built on TradFi foundations.
