## AI Data Center Copper Demand: A New and Growing Component

AI data centers require substantially more power infrastructure than conventional data centers due to their energy-intensive GPU compute requirements. The copper intensity implications:

Each 1 MW of data center capacity requires approximately 10-15 tonnes of copper in power distribution, cooling systems, and connectivity infrastructure. Large AI data centers (100+ MW) require 1,000-1,500 tonnes of copper each.

The AI compute buildout in 2024-2026 is expected to add approximately 50-100 GW of new data center capacity globally (estimates vary widely).
