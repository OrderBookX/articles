## What Is DeFi? The Decentralized Alternative

DeFi refers to financial services built on blockchain networks that operate without centralized intermediaries. Decentralized exchanges allow peer-to-peer token swaps. Lending protocols enable collateralized borrowing without banks. Yield farming protocols distribute earnings from liquidity provision. Synthetic asset platforms replicate price exposure to real-world assets on-chain. DeFi is characterized by open-source code, permissionless access, and trustless execution through smart contracts that cannot be altered without governance approval.
