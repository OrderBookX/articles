## Identifying Current Cycle Phase: The Phase Determination Scorecard

Use four indicators to determine the current cycle phase:

1. Yield curve shape (steep positive = early recovery, flat = late cycle, inverted = pre-recession)
2. PMI level and direction (below 50 and falling = recession, above 50 and rising = expansion)
3. Unemployment trend (rising = recession risk, falling = expansion)
4. Credit conditions (easing = accommodative for growth, tightening = headwind for growth)

Score each indicator for cycle phase alignment. The phase with the most indicator support is the current phase.
