Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) is a well-known investment strategy that involves purchasing a fixed dollar amount of a cryptocurrency at regular intervals, regardless of its price. This approach helps investors mitigate the risks associated with market volatility by spreading out purchases over time. DCA is particularly beneficial in the **cryptocurrency** market, where prices can fluctuate dramatically in short periods.
