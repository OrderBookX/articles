## Automotive Transition and Platinum Demand Dynamics

The electric vehicle transition creates a complex demand dynamic for bitunix platinum futures. As EVs replace gasoline and diesel vehicles, catalytic converter demand for platinum decreases over time. However, the pace of this demand erosion is gradual, as the global vehicle fleet turns over slowly and gasoline vehicles will remain dominant in many markets for years to come. Meanwhile, the substitution of palladium with platinum in gasoline catalytic converters, driven by platinum's historical discount to palladium, is supporting platinum demand in the near term even as the total ICE vehicle market shrinks.
