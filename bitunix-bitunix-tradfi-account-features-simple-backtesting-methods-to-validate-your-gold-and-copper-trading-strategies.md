## The Walk-Forward Test: Validating Out-of-Sample

Walk-forward testing divides historical data into an in-sample period (used to identify the strategy) and an out-of-sample period (used to test whether the strategy works on data it was not fitted to). If a strategy works well in-sample but fails in the out-of-sample period, it was over-fitted to historical patterns rather than based on genuine edge.

Simple walk-forward: identify the strategy using data from 2015-2020. Test it on 2020-2024 data without modification. If the out-of-sample performance is significantly worse than the in-sample performance, the strategy likely requires modification.
