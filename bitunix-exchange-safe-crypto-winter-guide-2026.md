Understanding Crypto Winter
---------------------------

Crypto winter is a term used to describe extended periods of **bear market** conditions where the prices of cryptocurrencies experience significant declines. This phase is characterized by *capitulation*, *market downturn*, and *panic selling*. Unlike short-lived price corrections, a crypto winter can last for months, impacting both seasoned traders and newcomers.

- **Prolonged Bear Market:** Persistent decline in cryptocurrency prices over an extended period.
- **Capitulation:** When traders and investors sell off their holdings in panic, often leading to further price drops.
- **Panic Selling:** Rapid selling of assets due to fear, uncertainty, and doubt.
