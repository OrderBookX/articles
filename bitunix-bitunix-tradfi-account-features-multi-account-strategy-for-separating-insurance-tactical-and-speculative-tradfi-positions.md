## Why Separation Matters

Without account separation, the psychological pressure from a losing tactical position can motivate reducing the insurance account position to free up margin for averaging down. This is precisely the behavior that destroys the insurance function: the insurance is reduced when it is most needed (during the losses that prompted the margin pressure).

Explicit account separation with different naming, purpose statements, and management rules prevents the tactical decision-making process from contaminating the insurance function.

## The Single-Account Alternative With Explicit Position Tagging

For practitioners who maintain a single account, achieve separation through explicit position tagging in the journal: every position is labeled as Insurance, Tactical, or Speculative at entry. The tagging creates a psychological commitment that substitutes partially for account separation.
