Introduction to Futures Trading
-------------------------------

Futures trading involves contracts to buy or sell a cryptocurrency at a predetermined price at a specific time in the future. This form of trading allows speculators to bet on the price movements of cryptocurrencies without owning the actual asset. Unlike [spot trading](https://www.bitunix.com?inviteCode=ab9nr3&utm_source=news&utm_medium=blog-article&utm_campaign=labsnews), which involves immediate delivery, futures contracts are agreements to transact in the future.
