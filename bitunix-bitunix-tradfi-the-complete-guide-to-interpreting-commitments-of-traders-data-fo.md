## Gold COT: The Managed Money Percentile as Primary Signal

Gold COMEX futures (100-oz contract): Look at Managed Money Net Position. Calculate the 5-year (260-week) rolling percentile rank. Below 25th percentile = room for new longs, favorable entry condition. Above 75th percentile = crowded long, caution. Above 90th percentile = extreme crowding, partial profit-taking warranted regardless of primary driver.

Gold Disaggregated COT bonus signal: PMPU (Producer/Merchant/Processor/User) net position. When PMPU is net long (producers buying rather than hedging), the physical market participants expect price appreciation   highest conviction supplementary signal.

## Copper COT: The Commercial Short Structure

Copper COMEX futures (25,000-lb contract): Commercial (hedger) net short position tells you more than managed money for copper. When commercials are at maximum historical net short (they have hedged the most production at current prices), it means large copper producers believe prices are high and have locked in current prices   a subtle bearish signal that the "smart money" has hedged its physical exposure.

Managed money copper net position: as with gold, percentile rank is the key metric. Below 30th = underinvested speculators (room for additions). Above 80th = crowded speculatively.

## Crude Oil COT: The WTI vs Managed Money Relationship

WTI NYMEX crude oil futures (1,000 bbls contract): unlike gold and copper where managed money extremes are the primary signal, crude oil COT interpretation requires comparing managed money positioning to the physical market signal simultaneously. When managed money is at the 70th percentile (moderately crowded) but EIA inventory data shows consistent draws (physical market genuinely tighter), the crowding is being supported by physical fundamentals   do not fade the crowding as aggressively as you would if the physical market were neutral.

Conversely, when managed money is at the 60th percentile but the physical market shows building inventories, the speculative positioning is above what fundamentals justify   high probability of managed money liquidation.
