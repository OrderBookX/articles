## What Circle Internet Group Actually Does

Circle is the company that issues USDC, a US dollar stablecoin. USDC is a digital token where each unit is backed by one dollar held in cash or short-term US Treasury bills. When anyone holds USDC, Circle holds the corresponding dollar in reserves and earns interest on those reserves. This reserve yield is Circle's primary revenue source.

Circle also provides payment infrastructure that allows businesses, DeFi protocols, and financial institutions to send USDC globally with near-instant settlement and minimal fees. This payment network creates additional revenue from institutional services and API access fees.
