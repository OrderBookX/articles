The financial world is no longer a simple binary between traditional and decentralized. ## The Three Models

TradFi is the established system of banks, brokers, and regulated markets. It provides stability, legal protection, and institutional trust, but at the cost of limited access hours, high fees, and barriers to entry. DeFi uses blockchain smart contracts to automate financial services without intermediaries. It offers transparency, permissionless access, and 24/7 availability, but carries risks from smart contract vulnerabilities and lacks regulatory protection. CeFi platforms operate as centralized entities that offer crypto services with some of the protections found in TradFi.
