The MOVE Index (Merrill Lynch Option Volatility Estimate) measures implied volatility in US Treasury bond markets, analogous to the VIX for equities. High MOVE readings indicate that bond market participants are uncertain about interest rate direction, which directly affects TIPS yield volatility and therefore XAUUSDT uncertainty.

When the MOVE Index is at extreme highs (above 140) and gold prices have not yet responded, the elevated bond market uncertainty is creating conditions where a TIPS yield move in either direction will significantly impact XAUUSDT.
