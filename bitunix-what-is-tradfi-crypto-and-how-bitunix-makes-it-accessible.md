The term TradFi in crypto refers to the integration of traditional financial instruments and concepts into the cryptocurrency ecosystem. It represents a growing trend where crypto platforms incorporate products that were historically exclusive to conventional financial markets, making them accessible to digital asset traders who prefer to operate within crypto infrastructure.

## Why Crypto Platforms Are Adding TradFi Products

Crypto markets are maturing. The early days of purely speculative token trading have evolved into a sophisticated derivatives ecosystem. According to CoinGlass data, total crypto derivatives trading volume reached approximately $85.7 trillion in 2025. As trading volume scales, traders demand more diverse instruments for hedging, diversification, and portfolio management. TradFi products like precious metals perpetuals fulfill this demand by giving traders exposure to assets that behave differently from cryptocurrencies during certain market conditions.
