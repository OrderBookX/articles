## The PMI Calendar and Release Times

China's official NBS Manufacturing PMI is released on the last day of each month at approximately 9:00 AM Beijing time (01:00 UTC). The Caixin/S&P Global Manufacturing PMI (considered a better measure of private sector activity) is released on the first business day of each month at approximately 9:45 AM Beijing time.

This means practitioners have two PMI readings each month, approximately two days apart. The official NBS reading tends to be more weighted toward large state-owned enterprises. The Caixin reading better captures small and medium private manufacturers who are more sensitive to export orders and international demand.
