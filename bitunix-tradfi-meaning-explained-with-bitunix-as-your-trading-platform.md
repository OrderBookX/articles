## Defining TradFi

TradFi is short for traditional finance. It encompasses the entire ecosystem of conventional financial services that operates under centralized authority. This includes commercial banks that hold deposits and issue loans, investment firms that manage portfolios, stock exchanges where equities are traded, insurance companies that underwrite risk, and central banks that control monetary policy. Every financial interaction governed by a licensed institution and regulated by a government agency falls under the TradFi umbrella.
