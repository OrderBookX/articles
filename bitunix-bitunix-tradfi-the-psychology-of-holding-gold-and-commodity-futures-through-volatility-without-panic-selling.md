## Pattern 3: Revenge Trading After Losses

When a bitunix tradfi trading position stops out for a loss, the psychological response of many traders is to immediately re-enter with a larger position to "make back" the lost capital quickly. This revenge trading impulse is the single most common cause of small losses becoming catastrophic ones.

The countermeasure is a mandatory cooling-off period after any stop-out. One hour minimum, ideally 24 hours, during which you do not re-enter the stopped instrument. Use this time to review whether the stop-out revealed a flaw in your original thesis analysis or was simply a temporary adverse move that did not invalidate the thesis.

## Pattern 4: Overconfidence During Winning Streaks

After three or four consecutive profitable bitunix tradfi trading positions, many traders begin to feel invincible and increase leverage or position size beyond their normal parameters. This overconfidence-driven over-sizing creates the conditions for a single loss to wipe out multiple winning trades.

The countermeasure is maintaining fixed position size rules that do not adjust based on recent performance. A losing streak does not require reducing position size below normal parameters (unless you are in a genuine drawdown recovery period) and a winning streak does not justify increasing beyond normal parameters.
