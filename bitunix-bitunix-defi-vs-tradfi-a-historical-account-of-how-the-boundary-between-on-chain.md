## The Origins of the DeFi vs TradFi Framing

When Ethereum launched in 2015, the dominant narrative in crypto circles was adversarial: decentralized protocols would replace banks, exchanges, and clearinghouses. DeFi was positioned as the existential threat to TradFi. By 2020, the DeFi summer demonstrated that billions of dollars could flow through autonomous on-chain protocols without any traditional financial institution involved.

Traditional finance responded with alarm. Jamie Dimon called Bitcoin a fraud. Regulators warned of systemic risk. The narrative of DeFi vs TradFi seemed to be heading toward a confrontation that would end with one dominant over the other.
