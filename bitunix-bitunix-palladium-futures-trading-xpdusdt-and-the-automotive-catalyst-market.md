## Electric Vehicle Transition: The Long-Term Headwind for Palladium

The structural headwind for palladium futures is the ongoing transition from gasoline vehicles to electric vehicles. Since electric vehicles do not use catalytic converters, every percentage point of market share gained by EVs reduces palladium demand. This structural demand erosion creates a long-term bearish narrative for bitunix palladium futures that must be weighed against the shorter-term supply constraints and current automotive production levels.

Traders who take a medium-term view on palladium must evaluate the balance between near-term supply constraints, which support prices, and the long-term EV transition headwind, which undermines them.
