## The Old Framework: China Construction Was Everything

Each square meter required copper in wiring, plumbing, and HVAC systems. As China's construction sector went, so went copper. PMI below 50 = bearish copper. PMI above 55 = bullish copper.

This framework worked because construction represented the dominant marginal demand driver. Analysts who modeled Chinese real estate accurately had a reliable COPPERUSDT forecast.
