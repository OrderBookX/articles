Leverage is not free money. It is borrowed exposure that amplifies both directions equally. The most common mistake new futures traders make is using maximum leverage because it is available.

## Advanced Order Types for ATOM Futures

Bitunix futures support order types that spot trading does not, and mastering them is essential for leveraged success.

Trailing stops follow the price as it moves in your favor, maintaining a fixed distance behind the peak. When ATOM reverses by the trailing amount, the stop triggers and locks in accumulated profits. This is the single most valuable order type for trend-following futures strategies.

Take-profit orders automatically close your position at a predetermined profit target. Combined with a stop-loss, they create a defined risk-reward bracket around every trade.

Reduce-only orders ensure that an order can only decrease your position, never accidentally increase it. This prevents errors during volatile markets where rapid price movements might otherwise trigger unintended position additions.
