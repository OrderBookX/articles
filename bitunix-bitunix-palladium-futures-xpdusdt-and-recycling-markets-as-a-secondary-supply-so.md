## Geographic Distribution of Recycling Supply

Automotive catalytic converter recycling is geographically concentrated in Europe, where EU end-of-life vehicle regulations require formal recycling processing, and in North America, where scrap metal industries have developed efficient collection and processing networks. Asia, particularly Japan and South Korea, also has significant recycling infrastructure for the large vehicle fleets in those markets. The geographic distribution of recycling supply affects the delivery logistics for recovered palladium into market, with recovery timelines affecting the speed with which recycling supply responds to price signals.
