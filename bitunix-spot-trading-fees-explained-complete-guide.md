### Understanding Spot Fees

The **spot fee explanation** is crucial for traders aiming to minimize costs. Spot trading fees at Bitunix are divided into *maker* and *taker* fees. Makers provide liquidity to the market by placing limit orders, while takers remove liquidity by executing market orders.
