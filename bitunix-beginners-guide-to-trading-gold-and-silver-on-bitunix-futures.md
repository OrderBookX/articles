## Key Differences From Crypto Futures

Precious metals respond to different catalysts than crypto. Interest rate decisions, inflation reports, dollar index movements, and geopolitical events drive gold and silver. These announcements happen on published schedules, meaning you can prepare for volatility windows. Add an economic calendar to your routine if you trade metals on Bitunix futures.
