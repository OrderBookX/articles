## How TradFi Perpetuals Work

A TradFi perpetual contract tracks the price of a traditional asset, such as gold or silver, and settles in USDT. The mechanics are identical to crypto perpetuals. You can open long or short positions, apply leverage, set stop-loss and take-profit orders, and hold positions indefinitely. Funding rates keep the contract price aligned with the underlying asset price, and margin requirements are calculated in real time.

## Why Precious Metals Matter

Precious metals serve as macro indicators and hedging instruments. Gold tends to rise during periods of inflation, currency debasement, and geopolitical uncertainty. Silver follows similar patterns but with higher volatility due to its dual role as both an industrial commodity and a store of value. Platinum and palladium respond to industrial demand cycles, particularly in the automotive sector. Having access to these instruments allows crypto traders to build positions that respond to macroeconomic conditions rather than relying solely on crypto market sentiment.
