## Interpreting Dashboard Scores

Total score range: -12 to +12. Interpretation:
+8 to +12: High-conviction long. Maximum size within risk management limits.
+4 to +7: Moderate conviction long. Half to full size based on specific setup quality.
0 to +3: Low conviction, lean long. Quarter size maximum.
-3 to 0: Neutral to lean short. Watch for reversal signals.
-4 to -7: Moderate conviction short or avoid.
-8 to -12: High-conviction short or full position exit.

## Weekly Dashboard Update Routine

Update the dashboard every Sunday evening using the prior week's data for all 12 indicators per instrument. Compare the current week's score to the prior week's score: is the signal strengthening or weakening? An improving score from +4 to +7 justifies increasing position sizes; a deteriorating score from +7 to +4 justifies tightening stop-losses.

The dashboard's primary value is not any individual weekly score but the trend of the score over 4-6 weeks: consistently improving scores identify building opportunities; consistently deteriorating scores identify positions to reduce.
