## Risk Management for Family Capital

Family capital typically requires more conservative management than individual risk capital. Keep the crypto DCA allocation as a small percentage of total family investments. Use only Bitcoin and Ethereum for the core plan. Avoid leverage entirely. Set clear rules about when the plan would be paused or terminated, such as if the total allocation exceeds a predetermined percentage of family net worth.
