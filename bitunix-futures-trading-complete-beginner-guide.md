### Risk Management Tools

- **Stop Loss Order:** An essential tool to limit potential losses by automatically selling a cryptocurrency when it reaches a predetermined price.
- **Take Profit Order:** Similar to a stop loss, this order automatically sells a cryptocurrency when it hits a target profit level.
- **Trailing Stop:** A dynamic stop loss that moves with the price to lock in profits while minimizing losses.
