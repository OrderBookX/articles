## The Halving Cycle Pattern

The historical pattern shows a predictable sequence. The halving reduces supply growth. Over the following 12 to 18 months, price appreciation accelerates as reduced supply meets growing demand. The market reaches an euphoric peak. A severe correction follows, creating the next bear market. Prices bottom approximately 12 months after the peak, and the cycle begins building toward the next halving.
