## Silver: Gold's More Volatile Cousin With Industrial Superpowers

Silver behaves like gold during monetary crises but has the additional characteristic of being a critical industrial input. Solar panels use silver paste as a conductor. Electronics use silver connections. Medical devices use silver's antimicrobial properties. This dual identity, monetary metal plus industrial metal, makes silver more volatile than gold and capable of larger percentage moves.
