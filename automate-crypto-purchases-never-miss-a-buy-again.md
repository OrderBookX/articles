-------------------------------------------------

The **Bitunix Crypto DCA** (Dollar-Cost Averaging) strategy is an advanced feature that allows investors to spread out their purchases over time, reducing the impact of market volatility. This approach ensures that you buy more when prices are low and less when prices are high, potentially lowering your average purchase price.

### Benefits of DCA

- **Reduced Risk:** By investing a fixed amount regularly, you mitigate the risk of investing a lump sum at an inopportune time.
- **Automatic Discipline:** DCA instills discipline by removing the need to time the market, which can be challenging even for seasoned investors.
