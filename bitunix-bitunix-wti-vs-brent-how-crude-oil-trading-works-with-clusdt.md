## WTI vs Brent: What Drives Each Forecast

The WTI crude oil forecast is primarily driven by US domestic production levels, pipeline capacity, and storage at the Cushing, Oklahoma hub. When Cushing inventories fall, WTI tends to strengthen relative to Brent. The Brent crude oil forecast, on the other hand, reflects North Sea production, European refining demand, and broader international trade flows.
