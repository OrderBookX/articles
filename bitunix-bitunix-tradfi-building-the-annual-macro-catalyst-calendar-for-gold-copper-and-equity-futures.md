## The Pre-Event Preparation Protocol

For each high-impact scheduled event, complete this preparation 24-48 hours before:

1. Current consensus expectation: what number or outcome is the market expecting?
2. Position sensitivity: how does each open position respond to the above/below/in-line outcomes?
3. Pre-planned responses: for above consensus, in-line, and below consensus outcomes, what specific actions (add, reduce, exit, hold) are appropriate for each position?
4. Level-setting: are current stop-losses positioned appropriately for potential post-event volatility?

## The Post-Event Analysis Protocol

Within 30 minutes of any high-impact release: assess the actual outcome versus consensus. Execute pre-planned responses. Document the event, outcome, and your response in the trading journal.

Within 24 hours: reassess whether the event changed the macro thesis for any open positions or fundamentally shifted the analytical framework.
