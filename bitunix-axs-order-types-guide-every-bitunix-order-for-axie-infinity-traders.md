## Limit Orders: Price-Targeted Entries and Exits

Limit orders specify the exact price at which you want to buy or sell AXS token. A buy limit below the current price fills only if the market drops to your level. A sell limit above the current price fills only when the market reaches your target. You never pay more or receive less than your specified price.

## Trailing Stop Orders: Dynamic Profit Protection

Trailing stops are the most sophisticated order type for AXS trend trading. They follow the price as it moves in your favor, maintaining a fixed distance behind the peak. If Axie Infinity reverses by the trailing amount, the order triggers and locks in your accumulated gains.

This order type captures the majority of trending moves while automatically protecting profits when the trend ends. For buy Axie Infinity coin swing trades and position trades, trailing stops consistently outperform fixed take-profit levels because they adapt to the actual magnitude of the move.
