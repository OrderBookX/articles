## Limit Orders

Limit orders execute only at your specified price or better. Use limit orders on Bitunix futures when you have a specific entry level based on technical analysis. Place your limit at support for long entries or at resistance for short entries. The advantage is price control. The risk is that your order may never fill if the market does not reach your level.
