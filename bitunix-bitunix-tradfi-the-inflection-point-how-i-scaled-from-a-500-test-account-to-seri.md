## The Psychological Challenges That Scaling Introduced

The single biggest unexpected challenge: position size in absolute dollar terms changes the emotional experience of losses even when the percentage loss is identical. A 5% adverse move on the $500 test account felt manageable. The same 5% adverse move on the scaled account created a dollar loss that triggered stronger emotional responses and a greater temptation to override the analytical framework.

The solution: never look at the dollar P&L during open positions. Track only the percentage relative to account equity and the thesis validity indicators. The dollar amount is noise until the position closes.
