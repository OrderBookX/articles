## How Traditional Finance Handles Custody

TradFi custody operates on several core principles. Client assets are segregated from company assets, ensuring that institutional financial difficulties do not affect client holdings. Multi-signature authorization prevents any single individual from moving funds. Physical security includes biometric access controls, surveillance, and geographic distribution. Regular third-party audits verify that assets match reported balances. Insurance coverage protects against theft, fraud, and operational failures.
