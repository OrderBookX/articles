## What Triggers Altcoin Season for buy Cosmos Atom

Altcoin seasons typically begin when Bitcoin has completed a major rally and enters a consolidation phase. Capital that flowed into BTC during its markup phase begins rotating into altcoins including ATOM as traders seek higher returns from assets with greater upside potential.

The Bitcoin dominance metric, which measures BTC market cap as a percentage of total crypto market cap, is the primary indicator. When dominance declines from elevated levels, capital is flowing from Bitcoin into altcoins. When dominance rises, capital is concentrating back into BTC. For ATOM crypto investors, falling BTC dominance is the green light for increased altcoin exposure.
