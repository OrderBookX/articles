## What Is TradFi's Bank of America Fund Manager Survey?

What is TradFi's most widely cited institutional sentiment survey? Bank of America's monthly Global Fund Manager Survey, which polls over 200 institutional investors managing trillions of dollars, asks direct questions about commodity allocations, risk appetite, and expected asset class performance. Key questions for commodity trading include: "Are you overweight or underweight commodities?" and "What is your current cash level?"

When the survey shows a historically high percentage of fund managers are overweight commodities (top quintile of historical readings), mean reversion toward neutral is likely, which is a bearish contrarian signal. When an extreme percentage is underweight commodities (bottom quintile), the contrarian long setup improves significantly.

The BofA FMR is published monthly and the summary is widely reported in financial media (Barclays, Reuters, Bloomberg cover the release). The detailed data requires subscription but the headline findings are freely available.
