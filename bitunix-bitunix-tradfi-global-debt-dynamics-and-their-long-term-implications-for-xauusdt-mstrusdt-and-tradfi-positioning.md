## The Global Debt Situation: The Numbers

Global government debt has expanded dramatically since 2008, accelerating further during COVID. US federal debt-to-GDP exceeds 120%. Japan exceeds 260%. Many European countries are above 100%. Across G20 economies, aggregate government debt-to-GDP has risen from approximately 70% in 2007 to over 100% currently.
