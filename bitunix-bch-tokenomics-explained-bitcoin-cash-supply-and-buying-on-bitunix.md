## Supply Mechanics and Inflation

BCH supply dynamics define the inflation rate that existing holders face. New token issuance through staking rewards, ecosystem incentives, or scheduled unlocks increases circulating supply over time. If demand does not grow at least as fast as supply, prices face structural downward pressure.

The critical question for any Bitcoin Cash crypto investor is whether the inflation rate is offset by sufficient demand growth. Projects with declining inflation schedules or burn mechanisms provide more favorable dynamics for holders compared to those with fixed high issuance rates.
