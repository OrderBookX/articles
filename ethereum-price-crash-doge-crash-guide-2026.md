Understanding the Ethereum Price Crash
--------------------------------------

In February 2026, Ethereum, like many other cryptocurrencies, experienced a dramatic price drop. This *price correction* was not isolated, as Bitcoin also plummeted over 50% from its $126K all-time high (ATH). The panic selling was further exacerbated by multiple exchanges freezing withdrawals, which led to over $2 billion in liquidations within 24 hours. The Fear &amp; Greed Index, a tool used to gauge investor sentiment, hit a low of 11, indicating extreme fear in the market.
