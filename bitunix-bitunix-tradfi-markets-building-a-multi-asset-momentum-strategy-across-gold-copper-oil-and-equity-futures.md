## The Core Momentum Logic for TradFi Markets

Commodity prices trend because fundamental changes (supply disruptions, demand shifts, monetary policy changes) take time to be fully absorbed by the market. A gold price trend that starts when the Federal Reserve begins cutting rates continues for months because the full impact of the rate cuts on the physical gold market takes time to develop. A copper price trend that starts when Chinese stimulus is announced continues as the stimulus flows through to actual manufacturing activity and inventory drawdowns.

This fundamental momentum is more reliable in commodity markets than in crypto markets because the underlying drivers have documented, multi-month transmission mechanisms rather than rapid narrative-driven sentiment shifts.

The momentum strategy can be enhanced by filtering signals with fundamental analysis: only execute momentum long positions in instruments where the fundamental analysis also supports the direction, and only execute momentum short positions where fundamentals also point toward weakness.
