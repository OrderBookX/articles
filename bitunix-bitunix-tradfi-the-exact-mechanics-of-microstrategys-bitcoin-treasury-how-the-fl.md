Tool 1   Convertible Senior Notes: MicroStrategy has issued multiple series of convertible notes (bonds that convert to equity at a premium to the stock price if Bitcoin appreciates sufficiently). These notes are typically 0% coupon (zero interest cost) with conversion prices set 30-50% above the stock price at issuance.

Mechanics: The company receives cash upfront (from bond investors) at zero interest cost, buys Bitcoin, and the bonds convert to equity only if the stock rises 30-50% from issuance.
