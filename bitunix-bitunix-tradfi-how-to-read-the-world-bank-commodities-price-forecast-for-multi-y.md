## The World Bank Forecasting Methodology

The World Bank's commodity price forecasts are based on supply-demand modeling that incorporates global GDP forecasts, energy transition projections, and mine supply pipeline analysis. The forecasts are not market-based (they do not simply extrapolate futures curves) but are fundamental-model-based   providing an independent view against which futures market pricing can be compared.

Free access: worldbank.org/en/research/commodity-markets (Commodity Markets Outlook downloads available for each quarterly publication, free with no registration required).
