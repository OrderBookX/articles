## Why DCA Outperforms Lump-Sum for Most Investors

Research across financial markets consistently shows that DCA produces better risk-adjusted outcomes than lump-sum investing for the majority of retail participants. The reason is simple: lump-sum investing requires you to be right about timing. DCA requires you only to be right about direction over the long term.

When you purchase ATOM token through DCA, your purchases automatically buy more tokens when prices are low and fewer when prices are high. Over time, this weighted averaging produces an entry price that tends to be lower than the simple average of all prices during your accumulation period. The mathematical advantage compounds as your time horizon extends.

The psychological advantage is equally important. DCA eliminates the paralyzing fear of buying at the top. There is no single make-or-break entry point. Every purchase is just one data point in a series, and no individual trade carries emotional weight.
