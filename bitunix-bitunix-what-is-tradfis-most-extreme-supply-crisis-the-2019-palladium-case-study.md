## What Is TradFi's Palladium Supply Structure?

What is TradFi's palladium supply reality? Palladium supply is uniquely concentrated: Russia's Norilsk Nickel produces approximately 40% of global supply, with South Africa contributing another 37-40%. This means approximately 78-80% of the world's primary palladium production comes from two countries, one of which (Russia) regularly faces geopolitical and financial disruption.

The supply deficit that drove the 2016-2020 bull had a specific cause: automotive emission standards in both Europe and China were tightened simultaneously, requiring higher loadings of palladium catalysts per vehicle. Palladium demand from this single application increased by approximately 1 million ounces annually   equivalent to 15% of total global supply   over a three-year period.
