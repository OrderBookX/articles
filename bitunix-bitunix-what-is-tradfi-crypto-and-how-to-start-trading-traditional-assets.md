## What to Watch When Trading Precious Metals

Precious metals respond to different catalysts than crypto. Key indicators include US inflation data (CPI and PCE), Federal Reserve interest rate decisions, US Dollar Index (DXY) movements, and geopolitical events. Gold typically rises when real interest rates fall, when the dollar weakens, or when global uncertainty increases. Silver follows gold but with higher volatility due to industrial demand factors. Monitoring these indicators alongside your regular crypto analysis gives you a broader market view.
