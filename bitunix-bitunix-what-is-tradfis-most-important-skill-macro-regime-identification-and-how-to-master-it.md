## What Is TradFi's Regime Identification Scorecard?

What is TradFi's practical tool for regime identification? A four-variable monthly scorecard:

Variable 1: GDP growth direction (rising = growth positive, falling = growth negative).
Variable 2: Inflation direction (rising = inflation positive, falling = inflation negative).
Variable 3: Central bank stance (accommodative vs restrictive).
Variable 4: Leading indicators (PMI, yield curve, credit spreads).

Score each variable +1 for positive economic environment, -1 for negative. Total score: +4 is pure boom, -4 is pure bust. Intermediate scores indicate which direction the regime is evolving.
