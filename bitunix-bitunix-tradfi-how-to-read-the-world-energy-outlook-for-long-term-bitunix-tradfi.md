## The WEO's Three Scenarios and Their Commodity Implications

The IEA's 2023 WEO presents three scenarios with dramatically different commodity implications:

Stated Policies Scenario (STEPS): assumes only currently enacted policies are implemented. Oil demand peaks around 2030 but remains elevated through 2050. Copper demand grows modestly. Announced Pledges Scenario (APS): assumes all nationally announced net-zero pledges are implemented. Oil demand peaks earlier and declines to below 50M barrels per day by 2050 (from 102M today).
