## The Chinese New Year Gold Demand Cycle

Chinese New Year (late January to early February) is the peak gold jewelry gifting season in China. The demand cycle follows a predictable calendar:

Retailer ordering period (November-December): Chinese gold jewelers begin placing orders with wholesalers and refineries 8-10 weeks before CNY to ensure adequate inventory. SGE withdrawals typically begin accelerating in mid-November.

Pre-CNY accumulation peak (January): SGE withdrawals are at their highest. The SGE premium to international gold prices typically rises to $15-30/oz during this period.

Post-CNY normalization (February-March): demand subsides as the gifting season ends. SGE withdrawals slow and the premium compresses.
