Strategies for Trading XAG/USDT
-------------------------------

Trading XAG/USDT requires a solid understanding of market trends and strategic planning. Here are some strategies to consider:

### Trend Following

This strategy involves analyzing market trends and making trades that align with the overall direction of the market. Using indicators like the *Exponential Moving Average (EMA)* and the *Relative Strength Index (RSI)* can help identify potential entry and exit points.

### Scalping

Aimed at capturing small price movements, scalping is a fast-paced strategy that requires quick decision-making and execution. Traders often use *Candlestick Charts* and *Order Books* to spot opportunities for quick profits.

### Hedging

Implementing a hedging strategy can protect against adverse price movements. By holding both long and short positions, traders can mitigate potential losses during volatile market conditions.
