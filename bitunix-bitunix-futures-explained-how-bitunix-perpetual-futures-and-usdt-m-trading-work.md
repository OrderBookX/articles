## What Are Bitunix Perpetual Futures

Bitunix perpetual futures are derivative contracts that track underlying asset prices without an expiration date. Unlike delivery futures that settle at a predetermined time, perpetual contracts let you hold positions indefinitely as long as you maintain sufficient margin. This makes Bitunix futures ideal for both short-term scalping and longer-term directional trades.

## Risk Management Tools

Bitunix futures provides take-profit and stop-loss orders, isolated and cross margin modes, and tiered risk limit systems. Isolated margin limits your potential loss on any single position to the margin you allocate. Cross margin shares your entire account balance as collateral, which provides more buffer but exposes your full balance to risk. For most traders, isolated margin is the safer default.
