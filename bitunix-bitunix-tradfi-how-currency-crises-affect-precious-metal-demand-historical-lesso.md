## The Geographic Pattern: Which Crises Affect XAUUSDT Most

Not all currency crises create equal XAUUSDT demand impacts. The determining factors:

Population size: larger country = more potential gold buyers. Turkey (85M) creates more demand impact than Lebanon (7M).

Gold purchase accessibility: in countries with active physical gold markets (Turkey, India, Southeast Asia, Middle East), crisis demand can be quickly expressed in physical purchases. In countries with capital controls or limited gold distribution infrastructure, the demand is suppressed.

USD alternative accessibility: when USD cash is easily available as an alternative to gold (as in many dollarized economies), the gold demand from currency crisis is lower. When USD is restricted or unavailable, gold becomes the primary hard asset refuge.
