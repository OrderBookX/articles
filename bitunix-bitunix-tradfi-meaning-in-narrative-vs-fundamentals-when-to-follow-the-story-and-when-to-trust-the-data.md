This narrative premium is the gap between where price is and where fundamentals alone would put it.

## When to Follow Narrative Momentum

Narrative momentum is most reliable for trading when: the narrative is in its early phase (before mainstream media coverage is widespread), the underlying fundamentals are directionally supportive even if not yet fully confirming, and the narrative has specific verifiable milestones that will confirm or refute it over the coming months.

The EV copper demand narrative in 2020-2022 met all three criteria: early phase before mainstream coverage, fundamentals directionally supportive (EV sales were actually growing rapidly), and verifiable milestones (monthly EV sales data and mine investment announcements).

## When to Trust Fundamentals Over Narrative

When narratives have been fully absorbed into price (mainstream financial media is uniformly bullish), when the verifiable milestones are failing to confirm the narrative (EV sales disappointing despite bullish copper narrative), and when physical market data contradicts the narrative (LME inventories rising despite copper demand narrative), the fundamentals deserve more weight than the narrative momentum.
