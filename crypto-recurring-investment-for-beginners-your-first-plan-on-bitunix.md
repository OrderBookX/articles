Why Choose Crypto Recurring Investment?
---------------------------------------

Crypto recurring investment, often referred to as *Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)*, is a strategy that involves investing a fixed amount of money into **cryptocurrency** at regular intervals regardless of price. This approach mitigates the impact of volatility and helps smooth out the average purchase price over time.

- **Reduces Volatility Impact:** By investing regularly, you spread out the purchase price over time, reducing the risk of buying at a single high price.
- **Automated Wealth Building:** Set up once and let your investments grow automatically.
- **Beginner-Friendly:** No need for in-depth market analysis or timing the market perfectly.
