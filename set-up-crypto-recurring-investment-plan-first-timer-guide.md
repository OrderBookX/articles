What is a Crypto Recurring Investment?
--------------------------------------

A **crypto recurring investment** involves setting up a plan to regularly purchase cryptocurrencies at predetermined intervals. This approach, often referred to as a **scheduled investment plan** or **systematic crypto investment**, allows investors to buy crypto on a schedule, regardless of the market price. This method is akin to dollar-cost averaging, where you invest a fixed amount at regular intervals, potentially reducing the impact of volatility over time.
