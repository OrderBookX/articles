## Understanding Slippage on ATOM Trades

Slippage occurs when your order executes at a different price than expected. Positive slippage means you get a better price than anticipated. Negative slippage means you pay more (for buys) or receive less (for sells) than the quoted price at the time you submitted the order.

Market orders are most susceptible to slippage because they execute immediately at whatever price is available. Limit orders eliminate slippage by definition because they only fill at your specified price or better. For large buy Cosmos Atom orders, slippage can be a significant hidden cost.
