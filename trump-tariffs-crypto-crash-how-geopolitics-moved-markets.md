Trump Tariffs Crypto Crash: How Geopolitics Moved Markets
=========================================================

The **trump tariffs crypto crash** of October 2025 stands as a stark reminder of how geopolitical events can dramatically impact the cryptocurrency markets. When former President Donald Trump announced a 100% tariff on Chinese imports, the *cryptocurrency* market was immediately sent into a **risk-off** mode, leading to significant **deleveraging** and an unprecedented wave of **liquidations**.

Understanding the Trump China Tariffs and Their Impact on Bitcoin
-----------------------------------------------------------------

The announcement of the tariffs had an immediate effect on global markets, but the **tariff announcement crypto impact** was particularly severe. The crypto derivatives market, which reached a historic $85.7 trillion in trading volume in 2025, saw a dramatic shift. Open interest peaked at $235.9 billion on October 7, but the subsequent crash saw over $70 billion wiped out within days. Bitcoin, often seen as a high-beta asset, experienced a 10-15% **drawdown**, demonstrating its volatility in response to **geopolitical risk**.
