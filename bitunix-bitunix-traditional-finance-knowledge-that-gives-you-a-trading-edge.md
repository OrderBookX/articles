## The Macro Connection

When the Federal Reserve raises interest rates, borrowing costs increase across all markets. Higher rates make safe-haven assets like government bonds more attractive, pulling capital away from speculative investments including crypto. Conversely, when rates drop or quantitative easing begins, liquidity floods into risk assets and crypto typically rallies. Understanding these TradFi dynamics helps traders anticipate moves rather than react to them.
