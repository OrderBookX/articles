## How Halvings Impact Bitcoin Price

When block rewards halve, miners earn fewer Bitcoin for the same computational work. This reduces the rate at which new BTC enters circulation. If demand remains constant or grows while new supply decreases, basic economics dictates price appreciation. Historical data supports this theory. The halvings of 2012, 2016, and 2020 were each followed by substantial bull runs within 12 to 18 months.
