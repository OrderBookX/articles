## TradFi: The System That Sets the Rules

Traditional finance establishes the macroeconomic environment in which crypto operates. Central bank interest rate decisions determine the cost of capital globally. Inflation metrics influence risk appetite across all asset classes. Bond markets signal economic expectations that crypto traders ignore at their peril. When the Federal Reserve speaks, Bitcoin moves. When employment data surprises, altcoin volatility spikes. TradFi does not just influence crypto indirectly. It sets the conditions that determine whether capital flows into or out of digital assets.

## DeFi: The System That Expands Possibilities

Decentralized finance created new financial primitives that did not exist in TradFi. Automated market makers, yield farming, flash loans, and composable protocol stacks opened possibilities that traditional systems could not replicate. DeFi innovation also pushed centralized platforms to improve by offering better rates, more transparency, and faster settlement. The competitive pressure from DeFi has made the entire crypto ecosystem more efficient.
