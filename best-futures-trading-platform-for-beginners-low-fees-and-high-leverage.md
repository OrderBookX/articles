-----------------------------

Futures trading involves contracts that obligate the buyer to purchase, or the seller to sell, an asset at a predetermined future date and price. This type of trading is particularly popular in the cryptocurrency market as it allows traders to speculate on price movements without owning the underlying asset.

### Why Choose Futures Trading?

- **Leverage:** Futures trading often offers significant leverage, such as **200x leverage**, allowing traders to gain larger exposure with a smaller amount of invested capital.
- **Liquidity:** The high *trading volume* in futures markets ensures that traders can enter and exit positions with ease, minimizing *slippage*.
- **Hedging:** Traders can use futures to hedge against price volatility in the cryptocurrency market, protecting their portfolios from adverse price movements.

The Advantage of Perpetual Futures
----------------------------------

**Perpetual futures** are a type of futures contract that does not have an expiration date. This allows traders to hold positions indefinitely, which is particularly advantageous for long-term strategies. The absence of an expiration date means that traders can benefit from continuous market exposure without the need to roll over contracts.
