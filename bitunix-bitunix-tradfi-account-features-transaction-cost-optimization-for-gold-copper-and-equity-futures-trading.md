## The Components of Transaction Costs in Perpetual Futures

Total transaction costs for any bitunix tradfi trading position include: the bid-ask spread (paid on entry and exit), funding rates (accrued during the holding period), and any platform trading fees. Bid-ask spread optimization: Use limit orders rather than market orders whenever possible. A limit buy order placed inside the spread (between the current bid and ask) captures a portion of the spread rather than paying the full spread to a market order. ## Reducing Round-Trip Costs Through Exit Discipline

Many traders focus on entry optimization but underestimate exit cost. A poorly timed exit (market order during thin liquidity, exiting at a level with high selling pressure) can cost as much as the entry. Exit discipline: use limit sell orders at pre-planned target levels set at entry, not market sells when price is approaching targets under excitement. A limit sell order at target collects the spread (or maintains it) rather than paying it.
