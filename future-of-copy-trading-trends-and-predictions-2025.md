Copy trading, a form of social trading, allows investors to automatically replicate the trades of experienced traders. This strategy democratizes access to financial markets, offering a pathway for novice investors to benefit from the expertise of seasoned traders. With the increasing popularity of *cryptocurrencies*, copy trading has become a pivotal tool in the crypto sector, enabling broader trader access across various platforms.
