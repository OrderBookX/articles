## What Triggers a Risk-Off Environment

Risk-off events fall into three categories with different durations and magnitudes:

Acute risk-off (duration: hours to days): Single unexpected events that create immediate fear: surprise interest rate changes, sudden geopolitical escalations, major financial institution failures. Gold typically spikes immediately; crypto falls alongside equities.

Cyclical risk-off (duration: weeks to months): Deteriorating economic data, tightening financial conditions, recession indicators. Gold trends gradually upward; crypto falls over weeks.

Structural risk-off (duration: months to years): Major monetary system stress, sustained geopolitical conflict, systemic financial fragility. Gold enters sustained bull market; crypto may experience extended bear market.
