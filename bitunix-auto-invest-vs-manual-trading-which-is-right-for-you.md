Manual Trading Explained
------------------------

Manual trading involves actively buying and selling cryptocurrencies based on market analysis and personal judgment. This approach suits those who wish to take a more hands-on role in managing their **cryptocurrency** portfolios.

### Advantages of Manual Trading

- **Flexible Decision-Making:** Traders can quickly respond to market fluctuations and news events.
- **Potential for Higher Returns:** Skilled traders can capitalize on short-term price movements, potentially increasing their *capital gains*.
 
### Challenges of Manual Trading

- **Time-Intensive:** Requires constant market monitoring and analysis.
- **Emotional Stress:** Decisions can be swayed by emotions, leading to potential losses.
