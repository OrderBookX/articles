## Why Permanent Insurance Works Better Than Reactive Hedging

Reactive hedging waits until stress signals develop before adding protective positions. By this point, the protection is expensive: gold has already risen, volatility has spiked, and the protective value of any new position is already partially priced in. Permanent insurance positions are established during calm periods when they are cheap and then maintained through market cycles.
