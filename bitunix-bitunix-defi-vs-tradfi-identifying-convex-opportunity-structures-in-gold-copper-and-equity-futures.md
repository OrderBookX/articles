## What Makes a TradFi Position Convex?

A convex position has limited defined downside and theoretically unlimited or very large upside. For bitunix tradfi pairs, convex positions arise from situations where: the fundamental upside is potentially very large but the timing is uncertain, the stop-loss can be placed at a level with high structural support creating a tight and credible risk limit, and the fundamental thesis has multiple potential positive catalysts that could accelerate the move.
