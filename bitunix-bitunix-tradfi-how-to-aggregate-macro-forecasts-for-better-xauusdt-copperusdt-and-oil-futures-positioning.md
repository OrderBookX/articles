## The Contrarian Signal When Forecasts Are Too Aligned

When all five sources for any macro variable agree strongly and the consensus is positioned at an extreme, the contrarian signal activates. If 90% of forecasters predict a specific Fed outcome and the market has already priced it in, the actual surprise potential is almost entirely in the other direction. Extreme consensus alignment is often a signal that the surprise will be in the direction nobody is forecasting.

## Using Forecast Revision as a Timing Signal
