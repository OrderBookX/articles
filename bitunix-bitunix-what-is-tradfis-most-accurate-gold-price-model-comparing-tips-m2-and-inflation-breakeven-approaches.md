## What Is TradFi's TIPS-Based Gold Model?

What is TradFi's TIPS yield model accuracy? Researchers have found that the correlation between 10-year TIPS yields and gold prices explains approximately 60-70% of gold price variance over multi-year periods. The model: when real rates fall 100 basis points (TIPS yield from 2% to 1%), gold typically appreciates 15-20% based on historical regression analysis.

The model's strength: simplicity, measurability, and consistent historical performance. The model's limitation: it explains gold's monetary investment demand but not its jewelry, technology, or central bank demand components. These non-monetary demand components explain why gold sometimes appreciates more or less than the TIPS model would predict.
