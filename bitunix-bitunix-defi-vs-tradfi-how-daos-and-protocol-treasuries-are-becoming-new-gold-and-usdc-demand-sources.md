## DAO Treasury Management and Gold Allocation

Several major DAO treasuries have faced the challenge that holding their native governance token creates circular exposure: the treasury declines in value when the protocol faces challenges, exactly when it needs resources most. This circular exposure problem has prompted leading DAO governance forums (MakerDAO, Uniswap, Compound) to debate treasury diversification into stable assets including stablecoins and potentially gold-backed tokens.

MakerDAO (the protocol behind DAI stablecoin) has already allocated billions of dollars of its treasury to tokenized US Treasuries and real-world assets as yield-generating reserves. If large DAOs begin allocating 1-5% of treasury assets to gold-backed tokens as a non-correlated reserve, the aggregate demand from DAO treasuries (collectively managing tens of billions of dollars) creates a new institutional gold demand category.
