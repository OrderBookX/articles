## LME vs CME Copper: The Key Differences

London Metal Exchange (LME) copper: The global industry benchmark. Trading occurs in pit-based ring sessions and electronic trade. Physical settlement via LME-licensed warehouses. The 3-month LME copper price is the reference price for most global copper supply contracts. Daily settlement at 12:30 PM London time. Open interest is dominated by commercial hedgers (miners, fabricators, traders).

CME copper (HG): Chicago-based futures market with different contract specs (25,000 lbs per contract versus LME's 25 tonnes). Predominantly financial and speculative participation alongside commercial hedging. Electronic trading nearly 24 hours. CME copper prices are closely tracked by US-based investors because of US exchange familiarity.
