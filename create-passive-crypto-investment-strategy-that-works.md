What is Passive Crypto Investment?
----------------------------------

Passive crypto investment refers to strategies that allow investors to benefit from the growth of the cryptocurrency market with minimal active management. Unlike **day trading** or **swing trading**, passive investing focuses on long-term growth with a *set and forget crypto* approach. It's ideal for those who wish to accumulate cryptocurrencies like **Bitcoin (BTC)** and **Ethereum** over time, aiming for *passive income crypto*.

Top Strategies for Passive Crypto Investment
--------------------------------------------

### 1. Crypto DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging)

*Crypto DCA* is a popular strategy where investors regularly invest a fixed amount of money into cryptocurrencies over a set period. This strategy minimizes the impact of market volatility by averaging out the purchase price of the cryptocurrency over time. It's an excellent choice for those looking to build wealth gradually without the need for precise market timing.

### 2. Automated Crypto Investing

**Automated crypto investing** involves using platforms that allow for automatic, scheduled purchases of cryptocurrencies. This approach not only saves time but also removes the emotional aspect of investing, making it easier to stick to your investment plan. Automated systems can include bots or apps that facilitate regular investing.

### 3. Staking and Yield Farming

For those interested in *DeFi* (Decentralized Finance), **staking** and yield farming provide opportunities to earn rewards by holding and lending cryptocurrencies. These methods can offer a stream of *passive income crypto* while supporting the networks they are built on.
