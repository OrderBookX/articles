## What Is TradFi's 3-2-1 Crack Spread?

What is TradFi's standard crack spread benchmark? The 3-2-1 crack spread represents the margin from processing 3 barrels of crude oil into 2 barrels of gasoline and 1 barrel of distillate fuel (diesel/heating oil). It is calculated as: (2 × gasoline price + 1 × diesel price - 3 × crude oil price) / 3.
