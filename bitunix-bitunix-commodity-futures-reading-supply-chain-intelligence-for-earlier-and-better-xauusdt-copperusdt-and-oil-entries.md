## Copper Supply Chain Intelligence: Treatment Charges as Leading Signals

The most valuable copper supply chain intelligence comes from treatment and refining charges (TC/RC), which are fees that smelters charge miners for processing copper concentrate into refined copper. When TC/RC fall to very low levels, smelters are competing intensely for limited concentrate supply, signaling tighter mine supply conditions. Low TC/RC historically precedes COPPERUSDT appreciation by 3-6 months.

Antaike (the Chinese Metals Industry Association) and commodity trade publications report TC/RC data quarterly. When TC/RC fall below $30 per tonne, the concentrate supply is genuinely tight and COPPERUSDT supply-side support is developing.
