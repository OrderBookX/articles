What is Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)?
------------------------------------

Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA) is an investment strategy where you regularly invest a fixed amount of money in a particular asset, such as **Bitcoin** or **Ethereum**, regardless of the asset's price. This method is particularly beneficial in volatile markets, as it reduces the impact of short-term price fluctuations and helps you avoid the pitfalls of timing the market.

- **Reduces Risk:** By spreading out investments over time, DCA minimizes the risk of investing a large amount in a volatile market.
- **Simplicity and Discipline:** DCA enforces a disciplined approach to investing, removing emotional decision-making from the equation.
- **Long-Term Growth:** Consistent investment in cryptocurrencies can lead to significant growth over time, capitalizing on market trends.
