## The Power of Correlation-Aware Diversification

Putting money in four different tokens is not true diversification if those tokens all move in lockstep. The real benefit comes from combining assets with different drivers. BTC responds primarily to macro liquidity flows and institutional adoption. BCH tracks Bitcoin but with divergences tied to payment adoption events. BNB is driven by BNB Chain ecosystem growth and platform usage. ATOM responds to interoperability developments and IBC network expansion.

When one of these drivers weakens, others may strengthen, smoothing your portfolio returns over time. This is not about eliminating volatility entirely, which is impossible in crypto, but about reducing unnecessary concentration risk that comes from single-asset exposure.
