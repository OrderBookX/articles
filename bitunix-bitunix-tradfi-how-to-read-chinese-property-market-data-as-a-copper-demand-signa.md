## The Property Market Data Sources

National Bureau of Statistics (NBS) of China publishes monthly real estate statistics (free at stats.gov.cn with English summaries through Reuters and Bloomberg China): floor space under construction (the best copper demand indicator   measures active construction sites), floor space completed (measures copper that has already been installed), new construction starts (leading indicator   new projects beginning require copper for foundations and early electrical work), and property investment growth year-over-year.

The most copper-relevant metric: floor space under construction. This measures the total stock of buildings actively being built   every square meter under construction requires copper for its eventual completion. When floor space under construction is growing year-over-year, the active copper demand pipeline is expanding.

## The Property Market Transmission Lag to Copper

The copper consumption timing in Chinese property construction: foundation work (minimal copper), structural framing (minimal), rough electrical wiring installation (peak copper   approximately 60% of total copper in a building installed at this stage), rough plumbing (additional copper), finishing electrical and fixtures (remaining 40%). The peak copper installation phase typically occurs 8-14 months after a project's construction start.

The forward-looking implication: when Chinese property new starts are strong for two consecutive quarters, peak copper installation demand from those starts arrives approximately 8-14 months later. This creates a predictable forward copper demand signal from property start data.
