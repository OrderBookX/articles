## DeFi's Alternative Transmission Channels

What is TradFi and DeFi's difference in central bank policy transmission? DeFi lending protocols do not use central bank rate policy as their primary interest rate reference. Instead, they use algorithmic supply-demand-based interest rates that can diverge significantly from Federal Reserve policy rates. This creates periods where DeFi borrowing costs diverge from TradFi borrowing costs, creating capital flows between the two systems as borrowers and lenders seek the best rates.

When traditional interest rates rise sharply, as in 2022, DeFi protocols may initially maintain lower effective rates due to their algorithmic structure, attracting capital from traditional borrowers seeking cheaper financing. Eventually, capital flows adjust DeFi rates toward equilibrium with TradFi rates, but the transition period creates specific arbitrage and positioning opportunities for traders who understand both systems.
