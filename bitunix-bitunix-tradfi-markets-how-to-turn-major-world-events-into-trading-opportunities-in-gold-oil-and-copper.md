## Event Reaction Management: The First 30 Minutes

When the event occurs, the initial market reaction is often an overreaction in the immediate direction, followed by partial reversal as the market digests the full implications. During the first 30 minutes after a major FOMC decision or geopolitical event, do not chase the initial move. Wait for the initial spike to stabilize before adding to or initiating positions.

If your pre-event position is profitable immediately after the event, consider taking 30-50% profits in the first hour to lock in gains from the initial move, then letting the remainder run through the post-event continuation.
