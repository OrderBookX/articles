## The Mechanics of TradFi Influence on Crypto

When central banks tighten monetary policy by raising interest rates, borrowing costs increase across the entire financial system. Higher yields on government bonds attract capital away from speculative assets, including crypto. Conversely, when central banks ease policy through rate cuts or quantitative easing, liquidity floods into risk assets and crypto markets tend to rally. This relationship has been consistent through multiple market cycles and shows no signs of weakening as institutional participation in crypto grows.
