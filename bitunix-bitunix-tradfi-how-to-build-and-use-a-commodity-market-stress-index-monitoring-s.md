## The Commodity Market Stress Index Construction

The Commodity Market Stress Index (CMSI) is built from six stress indicators, each scored 0 (normal) or 1 (stressed):

Indicator 1   US IG Credit Spread: Score 1 if above 150 basis points. Score 0 if below. Elevated credit spreads signal financial system stress that will reduce commodity demand via the financing channel.

Indicator 3   Gold-Copper Ratio Trend: Score 1 if the gold-to-copper ratio has risen more than 10% over the past 4 weeks (gold dramatically outperforming copper signals growth risk aversion). Score 0 otherwise.

Indicator 6   BDTI and Freight Rate Decline: Score 1 if the BDTI has declined more than 15% over 4 weeks (shipping demand declining, global trade softening). Score 0 otherwise.
