## Investment Grade Credit Spreads: The Economy's Risk Barometer

Investment grade corporate bond spreads measure the additional yield investors demand to hold corporate bonds over risk-free Treasuries. When IG spreads are narrow, credit markets are confident in corporate America's ability to service debt, implying favorable economic conditions. When IG spreads widen, credit markets are pricing in increased recession or distress risk.

## Emerging Market Dollar Debt Spreads
