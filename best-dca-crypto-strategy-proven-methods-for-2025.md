Understanding Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)
-----------------------------------------

DCA is a popular investment strategy where investors divide their total investment across periodic purchases of a target asset to reduce the impact of volatility. This approach is particularly effective in highly volatile markets like cryptocurrencies.

### Why DCA is Effective for Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies such as **Bitcoin** and **Ethereum** are notorious for their price swings. DCA helps investors avoid the pitfalls of market timing by spreading their investments over time, thus averaging out the purchase cost. This strategy is optimal for assets with more than 50% annual volatility.
