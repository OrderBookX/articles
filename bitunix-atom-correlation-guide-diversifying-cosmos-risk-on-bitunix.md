## What Correlation Means for buy Cosmos Atom Investors

Correlation measures how closely two assets move together. Perfectly correlated assets (correlation of 1.0) move in lockstep, providing zero diversification benefit. Uncorrelated assets (correlation near 0) move independently, reducing portfolio volatility when combined. Negatively correlated assets (correlation below 0) move in opposite directions, providing maximum diversification benefit.

ATOM has its own correlation profile relative to Bitcoin, other altcoins, traditional equities, bonds, gold, and commodities. Understanding these relationships helps you determine how much ATOM crypto to add and what it replaces or complements in your existing portfolio.
