Framework 2   Thesis Completion Exit:

Framework 3   Trailing Stop Exit (For Extended Trends):

When a position has moved significantly in the thesis direction and the fundamental driver shows no sign of reversal, a trailing stop converts the position from targeted exit to trend-following. The trailing stop moves up behind the price at a defined distance (typically equal to the original stop-loss percentage from the entry).
