Understanding Liquidation Cascade
---------------------------------

A **liquidation cascade** occurs when a series of forced liquidations, often triggered by margin calls, leads to a significant drop in asset prices. This phenomenon is typically observed during a **bear market** or periods of extreme *price correction* and *panic selling*. As traders' positions are liquidated, the selling pressure increases, potentially leading to further market declines or *capitulation*.
