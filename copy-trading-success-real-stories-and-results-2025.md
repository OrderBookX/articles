Understanding Crypto Copy Trading
---------------------------------

**Crypto copy trading** involves mirroring the trades of experienced traders in real-time. This strategy not only enables traders to benefit from the expertise of others but also helps in understanding complex market dynamics. Successful copiers focus on *risk-adjusted returns* rather than just profits, making diversification across multiple traders a key factor for success.

- **Learning from Trades:** Observing and understanding the trades of experienced traders accelerates personal trading development.
