## Earnings Day: Reaction Management

When earnings release, the initial market reaction in the first 15 minutes is often an overreaction. Resist the urge to add to winning pre-earnings positions during the first 15 minutes. Wait for the initial volatility to settle, then assess whether the underlying thesis was confirmed or contradicted by the actual results.

If results confirmed thesis: add to position after 15-minute settlement, bringing total to full size.
If results contradicted thesis: exit the pre-earnings position immediately, accept the loss, and reassess.
