Step 2 (4 minutes): for each instrument, extract three numbers from the current week's data: (1) Managed Money (hedge fund) net long contracts = long contracts minus short contracts, (2) Producer/User net position (commercial hedgers), and (3) total open interest.

## What COT Cannot Tell You

COT is backward-looking by one full week (Tuesday data released Friday   four days old). In fast-moving markets, positions change significantly between the data snapshot and the release. COT is most reliable during slow-trending markets   less reliable during rapid fundamental shifts. Cross-validate COT with funding rates (which are real-time) for current positioning confirmation.
