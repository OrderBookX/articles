When inflation falls from high levels, real rates rise as nominal rates remain elevated. XAUUSDT initially suffers. CRCLUSDT suffers (backing yield falls with rates). Industrial commodities may benefit if the falling inflation reflects demand-supply normalization rather than demand collapse.
