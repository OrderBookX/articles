## What Is TradFi's Founding Moment?

What is TradFi's origin story? On August 15, 1971, US President Richard Nixon announced that the United States would no longer convert US dollars to gold at the fixed rate of $35 per ounce. With that single decision, the post-World War II monetary order built at Bretton Woods in 1944 ended. Every currency that had been pegged to the dollar, which was itself pegged to gold, became a free-floating fiat currency overnight.

The immediate consequence: gold was no longer artificially capped at $35. By 1980, it had reached $850   a 24-fold increase in nine years. Silver rose from $1.29 to $50. Commodity prices in general surged as the dollar debasement that had been suppressed by the gold peg was suddenly free to express itself.
