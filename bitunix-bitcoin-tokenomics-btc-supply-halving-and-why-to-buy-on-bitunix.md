## Supply Mechanics and Inflation

BTC supply dynamics define the inflation rate that existing holders face. New token issuance through staking rewards, ecosystem incentives, or scheduled unlocks increases circulating supply over time. If demand does not grow at least as fast as supply, prices face structural downward pressure.

The critical question for any Bitcoin buy online investor is whether the inflation rate is offset by sufficient demand growth. Projects with declining inflation schedules or burn mechanisms provide more favorable dynamics for holders compared to those with fixed high issuance rates.

## Distribution, Vesting, and Whale Concentration

Token distribution affects governance power, price stability, and selling pressure. Concentrated holdings in team wallets or early investor accounts create overhang risk when vesting schedules unlock these tokens for sale.

Before you purchase Bitcoin, check the vesting timeline for major token allocations. Upcoming large unlocks can create predictable selling pressure that informed traders anticipate and position around.

## Demand Sources Beyond Speculation
