What is a Liquidation Cascade?
------------------------------

A **liquidation cascade** occurs when a series of leveraged positions are liquidated in quick succession, often due to a sudden market downturn. This can lead to *panic selling* and exacerbate a **bear market** environment, resulting in significant price corrections and *capitulation* events. Understanding this phenomenon is essential for traders who utilize **[margin trading](https://www.bitunix.com/register?vipCode=BITUNIXBONUS&utm_source=3rdparty&utm_medium=blog-article&utm_campaign=labsnews)** and leverage, especially on platforms offering high leverage, like Bitunix.
