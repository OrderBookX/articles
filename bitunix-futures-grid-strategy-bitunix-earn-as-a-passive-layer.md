## The Grid + Earn Workflow

Your grid capital is divided into three buckets. Active: capital currently deployed in running grid bots. Reserve: capital waiting for the next deployment opportunity. Earn: capital that isn't needed for immediate deployment, generating yield in Bitunix Earn.

### Rotation Strategy

When you stop a grid bot and returns capital to spot, immediately assess: is there a new grid opportunity? If yes, redeploy. If not, move the capital to Earn until conditions improve. When a new consolidation pattern emerges, withdraw from Earn and deploy a new grid.
