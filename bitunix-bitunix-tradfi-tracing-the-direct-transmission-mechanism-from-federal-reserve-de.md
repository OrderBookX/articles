## The Four-Stage Transmission Mechanism

Stage 1: FOMC Decision (Day 0). The FOMC announces its rate decision and releases the accompanying statement. The primary XAUUSDT response occurs within minutes of the statement release. The market reads the statement for forward guidance signals: is the Fed more or less hawkish than expected?

Stage 2: Real Yield Adjustment (Days 1-7). TIPS yields adjust to reflect the new policy direction implied by the FOMC decision. This adjustment is more gradual than the initial reaction because it involves the re-pricing of the entire Treasury inflation-protected securities market   a process that takes multiple trading sessions.

Stage 3: Dollar Adjustment (Days 3-14). The DXY adjusts to reflect both the interest rate differential changes implied by the FOMC decision and the foreign exchange market's assessment of the Fed's policy trajectory. Dollar adjustment is even more gradual than TIPS yield adjustment because forex markets incorporate global capital flow expectations.

Stage 4: XAUUSDT Full Pricing (Days 7-21). Gold's price fully adjusts to incorporate the new real yield level and dollar positioning. The full price adjustment to an FOMC decision is typically complete within 3 weeks, with the most significant moves in the first 48 hours and a gradual continuation thereafter.
