## The Bear Market Signal Sequence

Bear markets typically begin with the same signal sequence across commodity instruments: first, primary driver turns adverse (TIPS yield rising for 3+ weeks for XAUUSDT; PMI New Orders falls below 51 for COPPERUSDT). Then physical markets soften (LME inventory builds, SGE premium narrows). Then COT positioning unwinds from crowded long to neutral. Finally, price follows the fundamentals with a lag.

The practitioner who monitors primary drivers weekly catches the bear market signal 4-8 weeks before the price decline accelerates. The practitioner who monitors only price catches the signal after the bulk of the initial decline has already occurred.
