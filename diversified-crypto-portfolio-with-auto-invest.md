A diversified crypto portfolio involves investing in multiple cryptocurrencies rather than concentrating all investments in a single asset. The primary goal is to balance the risks and rewards by including a mix of established coins like **Bitcoin** and **Ethereum**, alongside promising *altcoins*.

### Why Diversification Matters

Cryptocurrency markets are known for their volatility. A well-diversified portfolio can cushion against severe market swings and protect against substantial losses. Here are some benefits of diversification:

- **Risk Management:** By spreading investments, one can reduce the impact of a single asset's poor performance.
- **Potential for Higher Returns:** Investing in a variety of assets increases the chances of owning high-performing cryptocurrencies.
- **Market Volatility Mitigation:** Diversification helps stabilize the portfolio's overall performance, even if individual assets experience volatility.
