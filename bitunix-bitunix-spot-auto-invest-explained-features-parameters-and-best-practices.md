## Price Range Filter

An optional but useful parameter is the price range filter. This lets you set a minimum and maximum price within which the plan will execute. If the asset price is outside your defined range at the scheduled time, the plan skips that cycle. This is useful if you want DCA behavior but want to avoid buying during extreme price spikes. Set a reasonable range based on your assessment of fair value, and the plan respects those boundaries automatically.
