## Take-Profit Orders: Systematic Profit Capture

Take-profit orders allow defining exit prices for profitable positions before they are reached, removing the emotional decision of "when to sell" from the profit-taking process. Systematic take-profit placement ensures that defined targets are honored rather than being modified upward by greed when positions are in profit.

The optimal structure: place the first take-profit order for 30% of position size at the initial price target. Place the second take-profit for an additional 30% at the extended target.
