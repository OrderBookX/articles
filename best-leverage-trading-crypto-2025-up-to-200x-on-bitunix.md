Understanding Leverage in [Cryptocurrency Trading](https://www.bitunix.com?inviteCode=ab9nr3&utm_source=news&utm_medium=blog-article&utm_campaign=labsnews)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Leverage trading** allows traders to open positions larger than their account balance by borrowing funds. This can amplify both gains and losses, making it a high-risk, high-reward strategy. As such, understanding the risks and rewards associated with leverage is crucial for any trader.

### How Leverage Works

In simple terms, leverage is the ratio of a trader's position size to their actual investment. For instance, a *200x leverage* means that a trader can control a position 200 times larger than their initial investment. This multiplier effect can significantly enhance profits, but it also increases the risk of **liquidation**, where a trader's position is automatically closed if the market moves unfavorably.
