## What Is TradFi's CFTC COT Report? The Definitive Institutional Positioning Tool

What is TradFi's most important free institutional intelligence source? The Commodity Futures Trading Commission's Commitment of Traders report, published every Friday afternoon at 3:30 PM Eastern time, showing positions as of the prior Tuesday. This weekly report separates market participants into three categories: commercial users (producers and consumers who hedge physical commodity exposure), managed money (hedge funds and CTAs), and other reporting entities.

For XAUUSDT analysis, the gold COT report (available at cftc.gov) shows the aggregate long and short positions of each category. Managed money extreme net long positioning (when hedge funds are maximum bullish) also historically correlates with near-term price peaks rather than continued appreciation.
