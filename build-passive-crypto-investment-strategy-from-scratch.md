Understanding the Basics of Passive Crypto Investing
----------------------------------------------------

Before diving into the specifics of building your strategy, it's crucial to understand what passive investing entails. Unlike active trading, which involves frequent buying and selling to capitalize on short-term market movements, passive investing focuses on long-term growth. This approach can be highly effective in the cryptocurrency market, where volatility is both a risk and an opportunity.

There are several benefits to choosing a passive approach:

- **Low Maintenance:** Once set up, a passive strategy requires minimal management, freeing you from the stress of daily market fluctuations.
- **Reduced Fees:** Less frequent trading means lower transaction costs, which can significantly enhance your *PNL* over time.
- **Long-Term Growth:** By holding assets for extended periods, you capitalize on the overall growth of the cryptocurrency market.
