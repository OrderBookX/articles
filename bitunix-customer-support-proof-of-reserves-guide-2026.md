What is Proof of Reserves in Crypto?
------------------------------------

*Proof of reserves* is a critical aspect of cryptocurrency exchanges that assures users of the security and availability of their funds. It involves third-party audits to validate that an exchange holds sufficient reserves to back all customer balances. This transparency measure is essential in fostering trust and confidence among users.
