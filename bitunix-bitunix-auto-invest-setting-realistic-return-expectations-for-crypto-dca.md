## Short-Term Returns Are Meaningless

Your first month of DCA will produce results that are entirely driven by short-term price action. If the market goes up 10% in month one, your plan looks brilliant. If it drops 10%, your plan looks foolish. Neither assessment is valid. DCA is designed to perform over 12 months minimum, ideally over 24 to 48 months. Judging it on shorter timeframes is like judging a marathon runner at the 100-meter mark.

## The Drawdown Reality

The investors who continued through that period accumulated the cheapest units that drove their returns in the subsequent recovery. The ones who stopped locked in their losses permanently.

## Compounding Consistency Over Time

The collection of hundreds of buys across a full cycle creates a position with a resilient average cost that withstands market volatility better than any single entry point could.
