## Copper: The Chinese New Year Restocking Pattern

COPPERUSDT has a specific and highly reliable seasonal pattern tied to Chinese manufacturing cycles. The period before Chinese New Year (January-February shutdown, dates vary annually) sees Chinese manufacturers and traders building copper inventory to maintain production through the extended holiday. This pre-CNY restocking typically creates above-average copper demand in November-December.

After Chinese New Year (February-March), manufacturing and construction activity restarts, creating another seasonal demand surge as depleted inventories are rebuilt. The post-CNY restocking period (February-March) is one of the most reliable seasonal tailwinds for COPPERUSDT.
