## Gold Physical Market Sources

World Gold Council (gold.org): Quarterly Gold Demand Trends report is the definitive physical gold demand database. Free download, comprehensive breakdown by sector (jewelry, technology, investment, central banks), geography, and trend analysis. Published April, July, October, January.

LBMA (lbma.org.uk): The London Bullion Market Association publishes daily gold price fixes and annual gold trading volume statistics. Their free publications include detailed precious metals market reviews.

Mining company quarterly reports: Barrick, Newmont, Agnico Eagle, and AngloGold Ashanti publish quarterly operational reports with production statistics, reserve estimates, and cost guidance that provide supply-side intelligence before it appears in industry-level data.

## Silver Physical Market Sources

Silver Institute (silverinstitute.org): Annual World Silver Survey provides the most comprehensive silver supply and demand data available, including detailed industrial application breakdowns. Free download.

Silver ETF holdings (iShares Silver Trust): Daily NAV and holdings data shows institutional silver demand in real time. Rising holdings indicate institutional buying; falling holdings indicate selling.

## Oil Physical Market Sources

EIA (eia.gov): US Energy Information Administration publishes free weekly petroleum inventory data (every Wednesday at 10:30 AM EST). The US crude oil inventory report is the most market-moving single weekly commodity data release. EIA also publishes monthly Short-Term Energy Outlook with supply/demand balance projections.

OPEC Monthly Oil Market Report (opec.org): Free download, published monthly, provides OPEC and non-OPEC production data, demand forecasts, and oil market balance analysis.

IEA Oil Market Report: International Energy Agency publishes monthly global oil supply and demand data. Free executive summary available to all; full report available to member country citizens.
