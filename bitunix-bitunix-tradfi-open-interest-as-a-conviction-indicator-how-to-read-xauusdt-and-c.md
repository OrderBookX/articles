## The Four Open Interest-Price Combinations

Combination 1   Price rising, open interest rising: New money is entering the market in the direction of the move. Buyers are initiating new long positions (not covering shorts). This is the most bullish configuration for XAUUSDT or COPPERUSDT   trend strengthening as new participants join.

Combination 2   Price rising, open interest falling: The price rise is driven by short covering (bears exiting) rather than new long initiation. While bullish in the immediate term, it signals that the move may lack sustainable fuel once the short covering is complete. Reduce position size or tighten stops in this scenario.

Combination 3   Price falling, open interest rising: New short sellers are entering the market aggressively. Bearish conviction is building   this is the configuration that precedes sustained commodity bear trends.

Combination 4   Price falling, open interest falling: The decline is driven by long liquidation (bulls exiting) rather than new short initiation. The selling pressure will self-limit when the weak longs have been flushed. This is often the capitulation configuration that precedes a price floor.
