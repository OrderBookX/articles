## ESG and Copper Mining: The Critical Minerals Paradox

Copper sits at the intersection of the ESG paradox: it is essential for the energy transition (the greenest metals application imaginable) yet its mining has significant environmental impact. This ESG paradox creates tension between the rapidly growing copper demand from green energy infrastructure and the ESG concerns about copper production methods.
